**I. COMMUNICATION RECEIVED**  
Sixteen days ago, the Ascendant Watch received an unprecedented communication from **Mechanus**, the plane of absolute law and order, through the celestial diplomatic relay network. The communication was transmitted by a **Modron Hierarch** (designation: Quintus-Septicon-7), speaking in the formal diplomatic register reserved for inter-planar crisis notifications.

The communication is reproduced below in its entirety:

---

*"To the stewards of the Material Plane designated 'Elydies Cluster' and associated celestial protective services:*

*"The administrative apparatus of Mechanus has detected a deviation in planar boundary integrity within your jurisdictional sphere. Specifically:*

*"The sustained operation of multiple bidirectional portals connecting the Abyss to the material plane, combined with the accumulation of Abyssal corruption in formerly neutral terrestrial zones, has produced measurable thinning of the planar membrane in the region designated by your cartography as 'central-eastern Stuik to western Mordrek.'*

*"Current membrane integrity in this zone: 74.3% of baseline.*

*"Projected membrane integrity at current degradation rate in 18 months: 52.1% of baseline.*

*"At 50% of baseline, spontaneous portal formation becomes statistically probable (p > 0.3 per month per 100 sq km). At 40%, planar overlay, a state in which Abyssal and material plane geography physically merge at the boundary, becomes statistically probable (p > 0.15 per month per 100 sq km).*

*"Mechanus does not intervene in mortal or celestial affairs. This communication is an advisory issued under Modron Administrative Directive 7.114.b.iii: 'Notification of planar structural degradation exceeding tolerable parameters.' The intended recipients are advised to address the root cause of degradation before threshold levels are reached.*

*"No further communications on this matter will be issued unless degradation exceeds 60% of baseline.*

*"End of advisory."*

---

**II. ANALYSIS; WHAT THIS MEANS**

### A. Planar Membrane Degradation
The "planar membrane" is the metaphysical barrier separating the planes of existence. Under normal conditions, it is effectively impenetrable: portals require enormous energy to breach it, and they heal when portals are closed. The Velvet Iron Army's sustained multi-portal operation and Abyssal terrain corruption are **eroding the membrane faster than it can repair**.

### B. Spontaneous Portal Formation
At 52.1% integrity, portals would begin **forming without construction**; random, temporary breaches that allow fiendish entities to cross planes without controlled portal infrastructure. This would render all portal-destruction operations ineffective; the enemy would no longer need built portals.

### C. Planar Overlay
At 40% integrity, the material plane and the Abyss would begin to physically merge in the degradation zone. The affected territory would become simultaneously material and Abyssal, subject to the physics, environment, and inhabitants of both planes simultaneously. This is an **extinction-level event** for any mortal population in the affected area ([[Dossier 01-0001.1468.2347]]: "Degradation Thresholds, Technical Explanation and Visual Modeling").

**IV. ROOT CAUSE AND REMEDIATION**  
The degradation is caused by:
1. **Active portals:** Each operating portal contributes a continuous drain on membrane integrity within its local zone.
2. **Terrain corruption:** Abyssally corrupted terrain (spawning grounds, portal anchor zones, and battlefield pollution) weakens the membrane passively.
3. **Soul traffic:** The transit of harvested mortal souls through portals produces a unique degradation signature not observed in other portal traffic types.

**Remediation requires:**
- Destruction of all operating portals in the affected zone
- Sanctification (via *Hallow* or equivalent) of all corrupted terrain
- Cessation of cross-planar soul traffic

Without all three, degradation will continue even if the rate is slowed ([[Dossier 01-0001.1468.4612]]: "Root Cause Analysis: Three-Factor Degradation and Remediation Requirements").

---

**V. IMPLICATIONS FOR STRATEGY**

1. **Clock:** The Mechanus advisory has introduced a **strategic clock** to the conflict. Even without the Velvet Iron Army winning on the battlefield, the mere passage of time with active portals degrades the plane to a point of catastrophic failure.
2. **Changed Calculus:** Portal destruction is no longer a tactical objective (deny enemy logistics): it is a **strategic imperative** (prevent planar collapse). This elevates portal strikes from operational priority to existential necessity.
3. **Mechanus Neutrality:** Mechanus will not help. Their advisory is precisely what it claims to be: a notification, not an offer of assistance. Mechanus's neutrality is absolute.

---

**VI. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Existential Priority:** The Mechanus advisory redefines the war's stakes. The Ascendant Watch must communicate this reality to all mortal allies: the war is no longer about territory; it is about the continued physical existence of the material plane in the affected zone.
2. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Accelerate all portal destruction operations. The Greyloch portal ([[Dossier 19-3500.5743]]) must be destroyed before operational status. The Dûrn Kordrek forge raid must be executed within 6 months. Terrain sanctification teams must be deployed to all former portal sites and battlefield corruption zones immediately. **[REDACTED]**
