**I. OVERVIEW**  
The **Lantern Corps** is the Ascendant Watch's field intelligence and light-intervention force; celestial operatives deployed to the material plane under strict detection-threshold constraints ([[Dossier 01-0001.11]]). Currently, **33 active Lantern operatives** are deployed across 7 operational zones.

**III. ZONE ASSESSMENTS**

### Stuik Eastern Front (6 Lanterns)
The highest-density deployment; reflecting the Stuik front's status as the primary active battlefield. Lanterns provide **near-continuous battlefield surveillance**, feeding real-time intelligence on infernal force movements to Stuik military commanders through mortal liaison officers. War-horn signal monitoring ([[Dossier 03-0378.6143]]) is a standing tasking.

**Assessment:** Adequately staffed for observation missions. Insufficient for intervention. Any combat engagement draws on the reserve.

### Silesbia Perimeter (5 Lanterns)
Focused on the Greyloch Forest portal construction ([[Dossier 19-3500.5743]]) and CDG sensor mapping ([[Dossier 01-0001.8211]]). LN-019 was lost in this zone ([[Dossier 01-0001.6141]]); the highest-risk deployment after the Stuik front.

**Assessment:** The Greyloch portal situation requires dedicated surveillance. Loss of another Lantern in this zone would create an unacceptable coverage gap.

### Ulbgar/Mordrek Plateau (4 Lanterns)
The thinnest deployment; reflecting the limited operational scope on the Mordrek surface. Primary tasking is monitoring Thornshade's communications ([[Dossier 19-3999.8638]]) and tracking the Sixth Stratum Beacon ([[Dossier 19-3999.4637]]). LN-044 discovered the beacon and remains the primary monitoring asset.

**Assessment:** Understaffed. If the Dûrn Kordrek forge raid proceeds, this zone will require significant reinforcement from the reserve.

### Suirx Territories (4 Lanterns)
Deployed for the active portal hunt ([[Dossier 01-0007.4737]]) following Operation Valkyrhino. LN-038, the operator who killed Arcanaloth "Fold" ([[Dossier 03-0378.4338]]), is the senior Lantern in this zone.

**Assessment:** Understaffed for the three-pillar portal hunting methodology. Additional assets would accelerate the search ([[Dossier 01-0001.3224.2301]]: "Zone Assessments: Coverage Analysis and Staffing Recommendations").

---

**IV. RESERVE MANAGEMENT**  
The 7-Lantern reserve serves three functions:

1. **Rotation:** Active Lanterns require periodic rest and spiritual renewal (typically 30 days per 180-day deployment cycle). Reserve Lanterns fill temporary gaps.
2. **Emergency Response:** Unexpected developments (e.g., a Balor sighting near a civilian population) require rapid deployment of capable assets.
3. **R&D Support:** Several reserve Lanterns support FOB Ashwatch's research programs, including CDG spoofing device development ([[Dossier 01-0001.8211]]) and Project Quiet Gate ([[Dossier 01-0001.3214]]).

Current reserve utilization: **5 of 7 are committed** to either rotation coverage or R&D support. Only **2 Lanterns** are genuinely available for emergency deployment at any given time ([[Dossier 01-0001.3224.3478]]: "Reserve Management: Commitment Analysis and Availability").

---

**V. FORCE SUSTAINABILITY**  
The Lantern Corps is a **finite and irreplaceable** resource:

- New Lanterns cannot be created on demand: celestial operatives of sufficient capability and willingness are rare
- Lost Lanterns ([[Dossier 01-0001.6141]]) are permanently subtracted from the force
- Detection threshold constraints prevent large-scale celestial reinforcement: increasing the Lantern count risks triggering infernal escalation

**Current attrition rate:** 7 Lanterns lost since campaign inception ([[Dossier 01-0001.6141]])  
**Replacement rate:** 2 Lanterns assigned to the theater in the same period  
**Net change:** -5  
**Projected force in 12 months at current rate:** 26 Lanterns; below the minimum viable deployment threshold of 28

([[Dossier 01-0001.3224.4612]]: "Force Sustainability: Attrition Projection and Minimum Viable Threshold")

---

**VI. PROPOSED REALLOCATION**  
Given current strategic priorities (the Mechanus advisory timeline; [[Dossier 01-0001.1468]]), the following reallocation is proposed:

- Move 1 Lantern from Stuik Western Front to Suirx Territories (portal hunt acceleration)
- Move 1 Lantern from Reserve to Ulbgar/Mordrek Plateau (forge raid preparation)
- Accept temporary increased risk in Reserve availability (1 emergency Lantern instead of 2)

This reallocation would maintain adequate coverage while accelerating the two highest-priority operations ([[Dossier 01-0001.3224.5738]]: "Proposed Reallocation: Risk-Benefit Analysis").

---

**VII. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Overstretched:** The Lantern Corps is operating at the limits of its deployment capacity. Every additional commitment degrades coverage elsewhere.
2. **Replacement Crisis:** The net -5 attrition rate is unsustainable. Without new Lantern assignments, the Corps will fall below minimum viable within a year.
3. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Approve the proposed reallocation. Escalate a formal request to celestial high command for 4 additional Lantern assignments to replace losses and support planned operations. **[REDACTED]**
