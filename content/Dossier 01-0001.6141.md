**CLASSIFIED: EMPYREAN CLEARANCE ONLY**  
**REF. 01-0001.6141**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Personnel Division  
**Subject:** Lantern Casualty Report: Seven Lost Lanterns

---

**I. OVERVIEW**  
Since the beginning of material plane operations against the Velvet Iron Army, the Lantern Corps has suffered **seven casualties**; operatives killed, captured, or missing and presumed dead. Each loss represents an irreplaceable reduction in the Ascendant Watch's field capability. This dossier records the circumstances of each casualty and identifies patterns for operational improvement.

---

**II. CASUALTY REGISTER**

### LN-003: "Starbright" (KILLED)
**Zone:** Stuik Eastern Front  
**Date:** 14 months ago  
**Circumstances:** LN-003 was conducting battlefield observation during the Battle of Korras Bluffs when the Balor "Ashterrex" detonated ([[Dossier 03-0378.4553]]). Despite celestial fire resistance, the shockwave proved lethal at close range: LN-003 was within 20 meters of the detonation, well inside the lethal shockwave radius.  
**Lesson:** Celestial fire resistance does not protect against detonation shockwave. Maintain 100m minimum distance from any Balor engaged by friendly forces.

### LN-007, "Dawnstep" (MISSING, PRESUMED DEAD)
**Zone:** Ulbgar/Mordrek Plateau  
**Date:** 11 months ago  
**Circumstances:** LN-007 was conducting deep surveillance of the Mordrek Plateau surface when communications ceased. No distress signal was received. Post-loss analysis suggests LN-007 may have been detected by a CDG sensor ([[Dossier 01-0001.8211]]) and ambushed by a rapid-response Vrock flight before escape was possible.  
**Lesson:** CDG sensor detection followed by aerial ambush is a specific and lethal threat pattern.

### LN-011: "Sunveil" (KILLED)
**Zone:** Stuik Western Front  
**Date:** 9 months ago  
**Circumstances:** LN-011 broke protocol to intervene in a civilian massacre; a Dretch column was overrunning a refugee caravan outside of Stuik's defensive perimeter. LN-011's divine intervention eliminated the Dretch column but generated a sustained celestial signature that was detected across the theater. An infernal response force; 2 Vrocks and 1 Marilith; was dispatched and engaged LN-011 before extraction was possible.  
**Lesson:** Protocol breach, however morally justified, carries lethal consequences. The detection threshold exists for a reason.

([[Dossier 01-0001.6141.1147]]: "Casualties 1-3: Detailed Circumstance Reports")

### LN-016, "Ashpetal" (CAPTURED, STATUS UNKNOWN)
**Zone:** Silesbia Perimeter  
**Date:** 7 months ago  
**Circumstances:** LN-016 was conducting Greyloch Forest surveillance when she was ambushed by the Greyloch Arcanaloth ([[Dossier 19-3500.5743]]) and a Mezzoloth guard detachment. LN-016's last transmission: *"Ambush. Containment ward. Cannot teleport. Cannot-"* Transmission terminated. No body recovered. The use of a containment ward suggests **deliberate capture** rather than kill.  
**Status:** If LN-016 was captured alive, she is currently being held and interrogated by the Greyloch Arcanaloth or has been transferred to Ziragz.  
**Lesson:** The enemy has developed anti-celestial containment wards. Any operative approaching known Arcanaloth positions must have telepathic escape protocols prepared.

### LN-019: "Glassfire" (KILLED)
**Zone:** Silesbia Perimeter  
**Date:** 5 months ago  
**Circumstances:** LN-019 triggered CDG sensor CDG-03 while conducting a routine sweep of the Greyloch southern approach. The detection triggered a Vrock response flight that intercepted LN-019 in open terrain. LN-019 destroyed 3 of 5 Vrocks before being overwhelmed.  
**Lesson:** CDG sensor CDG-03's position near the Greyloch approaches indicates deliberate placement to protect the portal construction zone. The area is now designated a CDG-hot zone requiring maximum stealth protocols.

### LN-024: "Embervane" (KILLED)
**Zone:** Preas Theater  
**Date:** 3 months ago  
**Circumstances:** LN-024 was targeted by a suspected Succubus assassination. While conducting surveillance of Vice-Cleric Asward ([[Dossier 19-3611.5423]]), LN-024 was poisoned through a contaminated water source. The poison, **Abyssal nightshade extract**, is lethal to celestials but harmless to mortals, indicating deliberate targeting. LN-024 died before the poison was identified.  
**Lesson:** The enemy knows individual Lantern operating patterns. Lantern operatives must vary routine, rotate observation positions, and verify all consumables.

### LN-033, "Hearthwind" (KILLED, TRAINING ACCIDENT)
**Zone:** FOB Ashwatch (Reserve)  
**Date:** 2 months ago  
**Circumstances:** LN-033 was killed during testing of a captured war-horn ([[Dossier 03-0378.6143]]). The horn's activation required channeling Abyssal energy; the resulting energetic backlash produced a lethal feedback loop that overwhelmed LN-033's divine protections.  
**Lesson:** Captured infernal equipment testing must be conducted by mortal operatives, not celestials. Abyssal-channeled devices have unpredictable interactions with divine physiology.

**V. MEMORIAL REGISTER AND RECOMMENDATIONS**

The seven lost Lanterns are honored in the Ascendant Watch's Memorial of Light at FOB Ashwatch. Their service and sacrifice are recorded for celestial posterity.

**Recommendations:**
1. CDG mapping is an operational survival priority: not merely intelligence collection.
2. Containment ward countermeasures must be developed immediately (LN-016's capture demonstrates an actively hostile capability).
3. All captured infernal equipment testing must be restricted to mortal operators.
4. Abyssal nightshade screening must be added to standard Lantern consumable verification protocols.
