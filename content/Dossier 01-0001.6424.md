**CLASSIFIED: EMPYREAN CLEARANCE ONLY**  
**REF. 01-0001.6424**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Forward Operating Base Command  
**Subject:** FOB Dawnspire: Operational Status and Threat Assessment

---

**I. INSTALLATION PROFILE**  
**Name:** Forward Operating Base Dawnspire  
**Location:** Concealed within a mountain peak 40 km east of the Preanese Theocracy's eastern border  
**Elevation:** 3,200 meters (above standard observation altitudes)  
**Concealment:** Enchanted to appear as natural rock formation. Anti-divination wards maintained at all times.  
**Personnel:** 4 Lantern operatives (LN-021, LN-022, LN-023, LN-025), 1 Subcommander (Oraniel), 2 celestial support staff  
**Primary Mission:** Intelligence collection and coordination for the Preas Theater of operations

---

**II. CAPABILITIES**  
FOB Dawnspire provides the following operational capabilities:

- **Arcane surveillance:** Long-range scrying operations targeting Preanese administrative centers. Current targets include the Light-Vicar's palace, the Sancrest Garrison, and the Tervane Cathedral district.
- **Shapechanger detection:** Passive divine-frequency scanning of the Preanese eastern parishes, identifying fiendish auras within a 30km radius. This scanning has identified 3 of the 4 confirmed shapechangers ([[Dossier 19-3611.3723]]).
- **Communication relay:** Dawnspire serves as the primary relay for intelligence products flowing from the Preas Theater to FOB Ashwatch. Transmissions use consecrated crystal resonance: a celestial communication method undetectable by CDG sensors.
- **Emergency extraction:** The FOB maintains a pair of celestial steeds (Pegasi) for the emergency extraction of compromised mortal operatives from Preanese territory.

**IV. THREAT ASSESSMENT; FOB SECURITY**

### Threat 1: Discovery by Thraxxia's Intelligence Network
Dawnspire's location has not been compromised, but the Abyssal nightshade assassination of LN-024 demonstrates that the enemy is aware of celestial presence in the Preas Theater and is actively hunting. If a CDG sensor is deployed within 30km of Dawnspire, any active scrying or divine channeling could reveal the FOB's location.

**Risk Level:** MODERATE; increasing

### Threat 2: Scrying Array Power Instability
The scrying array draws power from a naturally occurring ley line intersection beneath the mountain peak. Over the past 6 weeks, the ley line has exhibited **fluctuating power levels**; periods of reduced output followed by surges. This is consistent with the broader planar membrane degradation documented in the Mechanus advisory ([[Dossier 01-0001.1468]]). If degradation continues, the ley line may become unreliable, degrading Dawnspire's primary intelligence capability.

**Risk Level:** LOW (currently); projected MODERATE within 6 months

### Threat 3: Phase Gilt Operational Impact
If Thraxxia's Phase Gilt plan ([[Dossier 19-3611.5573]]) succeeds in absorbing the Preanese eastern parishes, Dawnspire's 30km scanning range would place it inside infernally-controlled territory. The FOB would need to either increase its concealment or relocate; both resource-intensive operations.

**Risk Level:** LOW (currently); projected HIGH if Phase Gilt succeeds

([[Dossier 01-0001.6424.3478]]: "Threat Assessment: Three-Factor Analysis")

**V. INTELLIGENCE PRODUCTION**  
Dawnspire has produced the following classified intelligence products over the past 6 months:

- 47 surveillance reports on Preanese administrative activities
- 3 confirmed shapechanger identifications ([[Dossier 19-3611.3723]])
- 12 decoded communications intercepted from the Preas Cell ([[Dossier 19-3611.5573]])
- Mapping data for 2 CDG sensors in the Preas zone ([[Dossier 01-0001.8211]])
- 1 emergency alert (the Vassara Tribunal: [[Dossier 19-3611.9529]])

Intelligence production rate: approximately **12 products per month**; the highest per-Lantern productivity rate of any FOB in the theater.

([[Dossier 01-0001.6424.4612]]: "Intelligence Production: Six-Month Summary and Productivity Metrics")

---

**VI. RECOMMENDED ACTIONS**

1. **LN-024 Replacement:** Assign a replacement Lantern to Dawnspire immediately. Current 4-person team cannot sustain operations indefinitely without rest rotation.
2. **Ley Line Monitoring:** Install a ley line stability monitor to provide early warning of power failure. Develop a backup power source (consecrated crystal battery) for the scrying array.
3. **CDG Sweep:** Conduct an immediate survey for CDG sensors within 50km of Dawnspire's location. Any sensor within detection range of the FOB's divine channeling must be neutralized.
4. **Relocation Planning:** Begin contingency planning for FOB relocation in the event Phase Gilt succeeds. Identify alternative concealment sites at least 60km from the eastern parish boundary.
