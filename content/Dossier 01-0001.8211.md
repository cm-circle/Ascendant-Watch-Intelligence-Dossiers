**REF. 01-0001.8211**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Enemy Detection Countermeasures  
**Subject:** Thraxxia's Celestial Detection Grid: Known Sensor Array Locations

---

**I. OVERVIEW**  
Thraxxia has deployed a **network of arcane detection devices**, designated the "Celestial Detection Grid" (CDG), across the material plane theater. These devices are designed to detect the presence and actions of celestial entities, including Ascendant Watch Lantern scouts. The CDG represents a direct countermeasure to the Ascendant Watch's operational doctrine and must be mapped, understood, and circumvented.

---

**II. DETECTION MECHANISM**  
The CDG operates through **paired resonance crystals**; enchanted stones approximately the size of a fist, buried at depth or concealed within existing structures. Each crystal is attuned to detect the unique **divine frequency** emitted by celestial entities when they channel divine power or activate innate celestial abilities.

Key characteristics:
- **Detection Range:** Approximately 500 meters per crystal in optimal conditions
- **Sensitivity:** Can detect divine channel usage, *Guiding Bolt*, *Sacred Flame*, and similar radiant-energy effects. Cannot detect a celestial entity in passive mode (no active divine channeling)
- **Alert Mechanism:** When triggered, the crystal transmits an arcane pulse to a receiving station: the locations of which are themselves a priority intelligence target
- **Limitations:** The crystals are passive until triggered. They do not provide continuous surveillance; they are proximity-based alarms

([[Dossier 01-0001.8211.1147]]: "CDG Technical Specifications: Detection Mechanism and Limitations")

---

**IV. OPERATIONAL IMPACT ON LANTERN OPERATIONS**  
The CDG has had a measurable impact on Lantern scout operations:

- **Detection incidents:** 4 confirmed instances where Lantern scouts triggered CDG sensors, resulting in infernal response forces being dispatched to investigate. In one case, this led to Lantern Casualty LN-019 ([[Dossier 01-0001.6141]]).
- **Operational constraint:** Lantern scouts must now operate in **passive mode** within any area suspected to contain CDG sensors. Passive mode limits their detection capabilities, healing abilities, and combat effectiveness: they can observe but cannot act.
- **Mission planning overhead:** Every Lantern operation now requires pre-mission CDG avoidance routing, adding planning time and constraining approach vectors.

([[Dossier 01-0001.8211.3478]]: "Operational Impact: Lantern Mission Constraint Analysis")

---

**V. COUNTERMEASURES**

### A. Sensor Neutralization
CDG crystals can be located through careful arcane survey (they emit a faint conjuration-school resonance when dormant) and physically destroyed. However, destroying a crystal may trigger a "breach" alert at the receiving station: potentially worse than allowing it to remain in place.

### B. Sensor Spoofing
A theoretical approach: create a device that mimics the divine frequency detected by CDG crystals, triggering false positives at known sensor locations. Sustained spoofing would erode the CDG's reliability and eventually cause the enemy to disregard its alerts. This approach is under development at FOB Ashwatch ([[Dossier 01-0001.8211.4612]]: "Spoofing Device Development: Concept and Technical Requirements").

### C. Passive Operations Doctrine
The current field expedient: operate in passive mode within CDG-suspected areas. This is effective but significantly degrades operational capability.

### D. Detection Grid Mapping
Systematic survey to locate all CDG crystals, creating a comprehensive map that allows Lantern scouts to navigate around them. This is resource-intensive but provides a permanent solution ([[Dossier 01-0001.8211.5738]]: "Countermeasure Comparison: Cost, Effectiveness, and Risk").

---

**VI. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Counter-Intelligence Capability:** The CDG demonstrates that Thraxxia's intelligence operation is capable of developing purpose-built countermeasures against celestial activities. This is a unique and alarming capability.
2. **Escalation Concern:** If the CDG is expanded or upgraded (e.g., to detect passive celestial presence rather than active channeling), Lantern scout operations would be severely compromised.
3. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Prioritize CDG mapping and spoofing device development. Accept limited neutralization of confirmed sensors only when operationally necessary. Develop Lantern communication protocols that do not rely on divine channeling. **[REDACTED]**
