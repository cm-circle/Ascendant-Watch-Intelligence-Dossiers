**CLASSIFIED: EYES ONLY / UMBRA CLEARANCE REQUIRED**  
**INTELLIGENCE DOSSIER 01-0007.11**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Strike Operations Command  
**Subject:** Operation Valkyrhino: The Stios Portal Interdiction, Brysbil Citadel Assault, and Aftermath

---

**I. OPERATIONAL CONTEXT**  
The town of **Stios** is a mid-sized settlement (pre-operation population: ~6,200) situated on the eastern frontier of the **Suirx Trade Company Territories** ([[Dossier 19-3428.0001]]), approximately 40 kilometers west of the Tsastashan foothills. Before the war, Stios served primarily as a trade waypoint and livestock market. Its strategic insignificance made it an ideal node for covert infernal logistics.

Intelligence gathered over a period of fourteen months ([[Dossier 01-0007.3515]]: "Operation Valkyrhino: Pre-Mission Intelligence Summary") revealed that **Mayor Halverd Kreg**, a human merchant of middling political ambition, had been recruited by infernal agents to operate a **soul-amulet trafficking pipeline**. Jade amulets produced in the ruins of Ulbgar ([[Dossier 19-3999.11]]) were transported via concealed arcane relay through Stios and onward to the Abyss through a stabilized **micro-portal** hidden beneath the **Brysbil Citadel**, the town's fortified administrative center.

The operation's discovery was precipitated by an intercepted amulet shipment at the River Deri crossing ([[Dossier 19-3428.1897]]: "Civilian reports from Deri") and subsequent tracing of arcane residue signatures back to Stios ([[Dossier 01-0007.3662]]: "Arcane Trace Analysis: Deri-to-Stios Corridor").

---

**II. MAYOR KREG AND THE TRAFFICKING NETWORK**  
**Halverd Kreg** was assessed as a low-tier collaborator motivated primarily by greed and self-preservation. His recruitment followed the standard infernal coercion model:

1. **Initial Contact:** Kreg was approached by a disguised Cambion operative posing as a Suirx trade factor approximately two years ago. The operative offered Kreg a supply of jade amulets as "trade goods" with no questions asked.
2. **Escalation:** Kreg was instructed to construct a concealed chamber beneath the Brysbil Citadel to house a portable portal anchor. He complied, diverting municipal construction funds and employing a team of non-local laborers who subsequently disappeared ([[Dossier 01-0007.4892]]: "Missing Persons: Stios Municipal Workers, List and Last Known Movements").
3. **Operations Phase:** For approximately eight months prior to the assault, the Stios portal processed an estimated **1,200 jade amulets**, routing them to an abyssal receiving point.

In addition to amulet trafficking, the portal was used for limited personnel transfers, a minimum of **twelve infernal agents** are believed to have transited through Stios into the mortal plane for deployment to various infiltration tasks ([[Dossier 01-0007.1627]]: "Transit Signatures, Estimated Infernal Personnel Throughput").

---

**III. THE ASSAULT ON BRYSBIL CITADEL**  
Operation Valkyrhino was authorized by **Celestial Commander Galadren** ([[Dossier 01-0001.11]]: "Celestial Intervention Protocols") and executed by a **mixed strike team** consisting of:

- **Three mortal operatives** experienced adventurers recruited and briefed by the Ascendant Watch.
- **Two celestial observers** (non-combatant) tasked with arcane portal analysis and post-operation site assessment.

---

**IV. AFTERMATH AND TOWN SECURING**  
Following the detonation, **Celestial Commander Galadren** deployed a detachment of Lantern scouts ([[Dossier 01-0001.11]]) and established a temporary **Forward Operating Base** in Stios. Measures taken included:

- **Civilian Assessment:** Stios' population was screened for infernal corruption. 
- **Mayor Kreg's Disposition:** Kreg was interrogated over a period of six days. He provided actionable intelligence on his Cambion handler, transit schedules, and the identities of three mortal collaborators in neighboring towns. Kreg was subsequently transferred to celestial custody; his current status is **[REDACTED]**.
- **Site Remediation:** The crater was sanctified and sealed. Residual planar instability was monitored for thirty days; no secondary rifts emerged.

---

**V. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Outcome:** Operation Valkyrhino is classified as a **tactical success**. The Stios portal has been permanently destroyed, severing a significant amulet trafficking route.
2. **Concern, Redundancy:** Intelligence suggests the Stios portal was one of at least **three active trafficking nodes** in the region. The locations of the remaining two are unknown; identification is a Tier-1 priority ([[Dossier 01-0007.4737]]: "Active Portal Hunt, Eastern Suirx Territory").
3. **Concern: Escalation:** The destruction of the Brysbil Citadel generated significant attention. Thraxxia's intelligence apparatus ([[Dossier 03-0380.11]]) is almost certainly aware of the operation and may adjust tactics, including relocating remaining portals or deploying counter-strike forces.
4. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Expand the portal-hunting operation across the eastern Suirx territories and the Tsastashan foothills. Mortal operatives should be rotated to prevent identification. Celestial involvement must remain deniable. **[REDACTED]**
