**II. INTELLIGENCE BASIS**  
Operation Valkyrhino was authorized based on converging intelligence from three independent sources:

### Source 1: Arcane Detection
Lantern Scout LN-038, conducting routine surveillance of the Deri-to-Stios trade corridor, detected Class-III conjuration signatures emanating from beneath the town hall; Stios's central administrative fortress ([[Dossier 01-0007.3662]]). The signatures were persistent, intensifying during nighttime hours, and consistent with the operational profile of a stabilized portal.

### Source 2: Missing Persons Pattern
Analysis of missing person reports from Stios and surrounding settlements revealed that approximately **140 individuals** had vanished over a six-month period; a rate 800% above regional baseline. The missing were predominantly working-class adults with limited social connections: transient laborers, unmarried migrants, and refugee newcomers. The demographic targeting suggested deliberate selection of individuals whose disappearance would attract minimal attention ([[Dossier 01-0007.4892]]).

### Source 3: Mayor Kreg Intelligence
Intercepted correspondence between **Mayor Halverd Kreg** of Stios and an unidentified external contact, using a simple cipher, revealed references to "the merchandise," "nightly consignments," and "the contract's quota." Cross-referencing with jade amulet distribution patterns ([[Dossier 19-3999.9275]]) confirmed that Kreg was facilitating human trafficking operations through the twon hall ([[Dossier 01-0007.3515.1147]]: "Mayor Kreg: Intercepted Correspondence and Complicity Assessment").
