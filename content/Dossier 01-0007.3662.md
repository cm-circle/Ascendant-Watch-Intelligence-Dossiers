**CLASSIFIED: FOR CELESTIAL EYES ONLY**\
**REF. 01-0007.3662**\
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Arcane Trace Analysis Unit\
**Subject:** Arcane Trace Analysis: Stios-to-Halvek Distribution Corridor

---

**I. ANALYSIS OVERVIEW**\
This report documents a pivot in our trace analysis: rather than tracking captives *toward* Stios, we have mapped the **outward flow** of jade amulets from the Stios portal node. Evidence confirms that the town hall is the primary induction point for the amulets, which are being dispersed across the continent via a secure logistics chain. Crucially, this distribution network is oriented **primarily in a northern direction** and remains strictly **behind Velvet Iron Army lines**, avoiding any proximity to current frontline combat zones or Allied patrols.

---

**II. METHODOLOGY**\
Lantern Scouts LN-038 and LN-042 tracked the **high-saturation jade signatures** emanating from the Stios terminus. Unlike captive heartbeats, which are faint and biological, the "Jade Trace" is a persistent, low-frequency hum in the weave that acts as a beacon for celestial sensors.

The 18-day tracking period focused on identifying the **supply chain safety-envelope**—the specific route designed to keep these artifacts within territory controlled by the Velvet Iron Army.

([[Dossier 01-0007.3662.1108]]: "Arcane Trace Methodology: Jade Saturation and the Safety-Envelope")

---

**III. FINDINGS: THE NORTHERN INFILTRATION ROUTE**

### A. The Stios Origin Point (Town Hall)
The portal sub-level in the Town Hall radiates a signature consistent with a **Class-III production node**. Amulets arrive at this location in bulk shipments and are immediately crated for transport. Traces indicate a **three to twelve hour turnaround time** from portal arrival to caravan departure, suggesting a highly disciplined infernal logistics cadre.

([[Dossier 01-0007.3662.2247]]: "Stios Town Hall: Throughput Analysis and Turnaround Times")

### B. The Northern Supply Logic
Analyzed traces show a **90% directional bias toward the north**. The distribution avoids the western roads toward Stuik (where Allied patrols are densest) and instead utilizes the **Northeastern Riverine Paths**. 

Key transit hubs identified:
- **Deri-North Junction:** A secure river port where crates are transferred from heavy wagons to shallow-draft river barges.
- **The Obsidian Bridges:** Secure crossings within the occupied interior where Iron Army gargoyles provide air-cover for the caravans.
- **Halvek Terminus:** A junction town 160km NE of Deri that serves as the primary "Sorting House" for amulets heading into the deep interior.

### C. Security: Behind the Lines
The most significant finding is the **"Safe Margin Protocol."** The trafficking route never approaches within 50 kilometers of the Allied front lines. By keeping the amulets deep within the "Red Zone" (occupied territory), the enemy ensures that:
- Caravans are never subjected to Allied "Stop and Search" protocols.
- Distribution to the subverted Preanese administration (the "Bloodless Invasion") happens through internal, secured river-routes rather than contested land-borders.
- Any "missing" crates are investigated by internal Iron Army provosts, not local law-enforcement.

([[Dossier 01-0007.3662.3389]]: "The Red Zone Logistics: Safe Margin Protocol and Frontier Exclusion")

---

**IV. THE HALVEK DISSEMINATION**
Once amulets reach **Halvek**, they are split into smaller, low-profile packets. From Halvek, traces indicate the network spreads further into the interior, specifically targeting:
- The mining settlements of **Kravoss** (for laborer pacification).
- The eastern parishes of **Tervane** (for subversion of local clerics).
- Refugee camps in the **Pale Marches** (offered as "charity" to the displaced).

---

**V. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Strategic Hub:** Halvek is now identified as a higher-priority tactical target than previously assessed. It is the heart of the northern distribution branch.
2. **Detection Gap:** Our surveillance has focused on the "Frontiers," but the enemy is winning the war of dependency in the "Rear." 
3. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Shift Lantern Corps focus from border patrols to "Deep-Scout" insertion into the Halvek sorting houses. We must stop the amulets at the source in Stios or the sorting point in Halvek. **[REDACTED]**
