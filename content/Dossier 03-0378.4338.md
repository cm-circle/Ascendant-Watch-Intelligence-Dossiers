**REF. 03-0378.4338**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: High Value Target Tracking  
**Subject:** Arcanaloth Attrition Tracking: Confirmed and Probable Kills

---

**I. STRATEGIC SIGNIFICANCE**  
**Arcanaloths** (CR 12) serve as the Velvet Iron Army's specialist engineers, responsible for portal construction, maintenance, and all advanced arcane logistics. The Ascendant Watch estimates Thraxxia has access to approximately **8-12 Arcanaloths** ([[Dossier 03-0380.6152]]). Since Arcanaloths are Yugoloth mercenaries operating under contract ([[Dossier 03-0378.6752]]) rather than Abyssal natives, they cannot be replenished through spawning, each kill permanently reduces the enemy's portal engineering capacity.

Arcanaloth attrition is therefore the **single most efficient** strategy for degrading the Velvet Iron Army's logistical infrastructure.

---

**II. CONFIRMED KILLS**

### Kill 1: Designation: "Lens" (Confirmed)
**Location:** Stuik Eastern Front, Ravine of Storms  
**Method:** Celestial strike element engagement; 2 Lantern operators  
**Circumstances:** Lens was detected constructing a Class-II portal for Stuik front resupply. The Lantern strike element engaged at range using *Holy Smite* and divine lance attacks. Lens attempted to teleport but was struck mid-casting, disrupting the escape. Kill confirmed by recovery of corpse and arcane equipment.  
**Date:** 8 months ago  
**Impact:** Loss of the resupply portal forced a 3-week logistics gap on the eastern Stuik front.

([[Dossier 03-0378.4338.1147]]: "Kill 1: Lens, Engagement Report and Impact Assessment")

### Kill 2: Designation: "Fold" (Confirmed)
**Location:** Stios, Brysbil Citadel; Operation Valkyrhino  
**Method:** Task Force Rhino mortal strike team  
**Circumstances:** Fold was the portal engineer for the Stios Class-III installation. Fold **escaped** via *Teleport* ([[Dossier 01-0007.11]]). Arcane trace analysis of the teleport destination indicated transit to a location within 50km of Stios; not to an Abyssal safe haven. Subsequent tracking by Lantern Scout LN-038 located Fold attempting to establish a temporary communication link 32km east of Stios. LN-038 engaged and eliminated Fold.  
**Date:** 5 months ago  
**Impact:** Permanent loss of the Stios node's engineering capability. The remaining eastern Suirx portals lost their maintenance support.

([[Dossier 03-0378.4338.2301]]: "Kill 2: Fold, Post-Operation Pursuit and Tracking")

**III. PROBABLE KILLS**

### Probable Kill 1: Designation: "Spiral" (High Confidence)
**Location:** Northern Stuik Front, near Halvegård Tower  
**Method:** Unknown; possibly Stuik military engagement  
**Evidence:** Portal activity in the northern Stuik sector ceased abruptly 6 months ago. Subsequent arcane surveys confirmed the portal anchor in the area was non-functional and deteriorating; consistent with loss of the assigned engineer. No Arcanaloth body recovered, but the cessation of all portal activity in the zone provides strong circumstantial evidence.

### Probable Kill 2: Designation: Unknown (Moderate Confidence)
**Location:** Unknown  
**Method:** Unknown  
**Evidence:** Intercepted Yugoloth administrative communications ([[Dossier 03-0378.6752]]) reference a "contract adjustment for reduced headcount" approximately 4 months ago. This business notation is consistent with Yugoloth bureaucratic acknowledgment of personnel loss. The timing aligns with a period of increased Lantern patrol activity along the Ulbgar border.

**V. ATTRITION CAMPAIGN DOCTRINE**  
The Ascendant Watch has formalized Arcanaloth attrition as a standing operational priority:

1. **Detection:** Lantern scouts are assigned to continuously monitor all known and suspected portal locations for Arcanaloth signatures.
2. **Engagement Rules:** Engagement is authorized upon positive identification. Arcanaloth kills take priority over all other targets of opportunity except Balors.
3. **Kill Verification:** Recovery of the body or arcane equipment is required for confirmed kill status. Circumstantial evidence (portal cessation) supports probable kill status.
4. **Capture Preference:** When operationally feasible, **capture is preferred over kill**. A captured Arcanaloth can provide intelligence on portal network topology, Yugoloth contractual terms, and enemy logistics, information worth more than the attrition value of a single kill ([[Dossier 03-0378.4338.5738]]: "Capture vs. Kill, Value Assessment and Operational Guidance").

---

**VI. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Progress:** 2 confirmed and 2 probable Arcanaloth kills have reduced the enemy's engineering pool by approximately 33%. This is a significant achievement with measurable operational impact.
2. **Tipping Point:** If the remaining pool drops below 5, the enemy will be unable to maintain all existing portals simultaneously: forcing a triage decision on which portal nodes to maintain and which to abandon.
3. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Continue and intensify the attrition campaign. Prioritize the Greyloch engineer for elimination before the portal reaches operational status. Investigate capture opportunities for intelligence gain. **[REDACTED]**
