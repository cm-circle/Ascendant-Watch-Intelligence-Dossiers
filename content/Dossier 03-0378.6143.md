**CLASSIFIED: FOR CELESTIAL EYES ONLY**  
**REF. 03-0378.6143**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Signals Intelligence Division  
**Subject:** Infernal Battlefield Communications: War-Horn Systems and Tactical Signals

---

**I. OVERVIEW**  
The Velvet Iron Army employs a battlefield communications system based on **war-horns**; large, enchanted instruments carried by designated signallers attached to each Balor field commander. This dossier documents the system's structure, decoded signal library, and exploitation opportunities.

---

**II. SYSTEM DESCRIPTION**

### Physical Components
War-horns are constructed from the **bone of slain celestials**; a material that conducts infernal magical energy with exceptional efficiency. Each horn is approximately 1.5 meters in length, curved, and engraved with Abyssal command sigils. The horns are too large for most fiends to carry; they are typically mounted on wheeled iron carriages pulled by Dretch teams or strapped to the backs of Hezrou.

Each Balor field commander is assigned **two war-horns**; a primary and a backup. The total identified war-horn count across all observed Velvet Iron Army formations is approximately **80**, suggesting 40 active field commands.

### Signal Generation
The horns produce a **deep, resonant blast** audible at distances up to 10 kilometers in open terrain. The sound carries an embedded **infernal harmonic**; a magical component detectable by arcane-sensitive observers but inaudible to most mortals. This harmonic carries the signal's encoded meaning.

The combination of audible blast pattern and inaudible harmonic encoding creates a two-layer communication system: the blast pattern provides a general alert audible to all forces, while the harmonic carries specific tactical orders readable only by demons with innate infernal perception ([[Dossier 03-0378.6143.1147]]: "War-Horn Construction: Material Analysis and Signal Mechanics").

**IV. SYSTEM LIMITATIONS**

1. **Range:** 10km maximum. In mountainous or heavily forested terrain, the range drops to 3-5km due to sound absorption. The infernal harmonic is similarly affected.
2. **One-way:** War-horns are broadcast-only: they transmit orders but do not receive responses. Subordinate units acknowledge through visual signals (fire columns), runner, or their own horns.
3. **No encryption:** The signals are patterns, not encrypted messages. Once decoded, they can be anticipated by opposing forces. The Velvet Iron Army has not changed its signal patterns in the 18 months of observed operations: suggesting either overconfidence or doctrinal rigidity.
4. **Signaller vulnerability:** The horn carriage is a large, identifiable target. Destroying the horn or killing the signaller severs a field commander's ability to issue coordinated orders to forces beyond visual range.

([[Dossier 03-0378.6143.3478]]: "System Limitations: Range, Directionality, and Vulnerability Analysis")

---

**V. EXPLOITATION OPPORTUNITIES**

### A. Signal Intelligence
Monitoring war-horn signals provides **advance warning** of enemy tactical intentions. A Lantern scout positioned within 10km of an infernal formation can detect and decode horn signals, providing minutes of advance notice before enemy actions.

### B. Signal Jamming
Theoretical: a sufficiently powerful *Silence* spell cast on or near a war-horn would prevent all signal generation within its area of effect. However, the horn's infernal harmonic may partially penetrate standard *Silence* effects, testing is recommended ([[Dossier 03-0378.6143.4612]]: "Signal Jamming, Theoretical Approaches and Testing Requirements").

### C. Spoofing
The most promising exploitation vector: **fabricating false war-horn signals** to misdirect infernal forces. If a captured war-horn can be operated by a mortal sorcerer with sufficient Abyssal magical attunement, false signals could order retreats, misdirect Dretch waves, or trigger premature portal activations.

Two war-horns have been recovered from battlefield salvage. Current efforts to operate them have been limited by the requirement for infernal attunement, a mortal sorcerer must channel Abyssal energy to activate the horn's harmonic component. This carries **corruption risk** for the operator ([[Dossier 03-0378.6143.5738]]: "Signal Spoofing, Captured Horn Operations and Corruption Risk Management").

---

**VI. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Archaic but Effective:** The war-horn system is primitive by celestial communication standards but well-matched to the Velvet Iron Army's force composition: large, low-intelligence forces that need simple, clear signals.
2. **Exploitable:** The system's lack of encryption, unchanging signal patterns, and signaller vulnerability make it a fertile target for intelligence collection and tactical disruption.
3. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Deploy war-horn monitoring protocols to all Lantern scouts on the Stuik front. Fast-track captured horn spoofing research. Develop a *Silence*-based jamming capability for field deployment. **[REDACTED]**
