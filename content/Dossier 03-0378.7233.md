**CLASSIFIED: FOR CELESTIAL EYES ONLY**  
**REF. 03-0378.7233**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Infernal Force Generation Analysis  
**Subject:** Dretch Spawning Infrastructure: Confirmed and Suspected Locations

---

**I. OVERVIEW**  
The Velvet Iron Army's primary tactical resource is the **Dretch** (CR 1/4), the weakest class of demon, deployed in overwhelming numbers as expendable assault troops, manual labor, and attrition warfare assets. The Army's Dretch supply is sustained by a network of **spawning grounds**, Abyssal locations where Dretch are generated through accelerated demonic reproduction rituals.

This dossier documents confirmed and suspected spawning ground locations, production capacity estimates, and interdiction opportunities.

---

**II. DRETCH PRODUCTION BIOLOGY**  
Dretches are not "born" in the mortal sense. They are **coalesced** from the ambient chaotic energy of the Abyss: a process that occurs naturally but slowly (one Dretch per approximately 100 square meters of corrupted Abyssal terrain per month). Spawning grounds accelerate this process through:

- **Ritual circles** that concentrate Abyssal energy into focused coalescing points
- **Soul catalysts**: harvested mortal soul energy ([[Dossier 19-3999.9275]]) that dramatically accelerates the coalescing process. One fully charged jade amulet provides sufficient soul energy to catalyze approximately 20 Dretch from prepared spawning ground terrain.
- **Hezrou overseers**: Hezrou possess a natural affinity with the Dretch spawning process and serve as "midwives," guiding the coalescing energy into viable Dretch forms. Without Hezrou oversight, approximately 30% of accelerated spawns produce non-viable forms that dissolve within hours.

([[Dossier 03-0378.7233.1147]]: "Dretch Coalescing Biology: Natural vs. Accelerated Production")

**III. CONFIRMED SPAWNING GROUNDS**

### Location Alpha: Occupied Ulbgar, Stratum 7
**Status:** Active  
**Evidence:** Operative Thornshade ([[Dossier 19-3999.8638]]) observed Dretch manual labor forces in Dûrn Kordrek being replenished at a rate inconsistent with portal importation. The deepest stratum of the collapsed kingdom, now fully Abyssal-corrupted, has been converted to a spawning ground.  
**Production Estimate:** 500-800 Dretch per month  
**Oversight:** 4 Hezrou overseers confirmed by Thornshade's thermal detection

### Location Beta: Abyssal Staging Zone (Graz'zt's Layer)
**Status:** Active  
**Evidence:** Portal transit signatures ([[Dossier 03-0380.6152]]) include massive one-way Dretch flows from an Abyssal-side portal designated "Spawning Grounds" in intercepted logistics communications. This is the primary production facility.  
**Production Estimate:** 2,000-5,000 Dretch per month (based on transit volume analysis)  
**Oversight:** Unknown; Abyssal-side observation is not currently possible

([[Dossier 03-0378.7233.2301]]: "Confirmed Spawning Grounds: Location Details and Production Estimates")

**IV. SUSPECTED SPAWNING GROUNDS**

### Location Gamma: Greyloch Forest Sub-Surface
If the Greyloch portal ([[Dossier 19-3500.5743]]) reaches operational status, the Abyssal-corrupted zone around the portal anchor could be developed into a material-plane spawning ground. Pre-corruption of the local terrain is already underway; the wildlife displacement and atmospheric anomalies reported by forest wardens are consistent with early-stage Abyssal corruption.

**Timeline:** 4-8 months after portal activation before spawning ground becomes productive

### Location Delta: Former Battlefields (Stuik Front)
The sustained infernal presence on the Stuik front has created pockets of Abyssal corruption in areas of prolonged combat; particularly sites where large numbers of fiends have died. These corrupted zones may spontaneously generate low-quality Dretches without formal spawning ground infrastructure.

**Production Estimate:** Minimal (50-100 per month across all zones), but significant as a supplemental source that requires no logistical support

**V. STRATEGIC IMPLICATIONS**

1. **Infinite Attrition:** The Dretch spawning infrastructure ensures that the Velvet Iron Army can replace losses faster than any mortal force can inflict them. Traditional attrition warfare is unwinnable.
2. **Soul-Catalyst Dependency:** Accelerated spawning depends on harvested soul energy. Disrupting the jade amulet pipeline ([[Dossier 19-3999.9275]]) would reduce spawning rates by an estimated 60-70%, converting accelerated production back to natural-rate coalescing.
3. **Hezrou Vulnerability:** Eliminating Hezrou overseers would reduce spawning quality: increasing non-viable spawn rates from ~0% (with oversight) to ~30%, effectively reducing productive output by a third.
4. **Terrain Corruption:** Any Abyssal portal that operates for extended periods will corrupt surrounding terrain, potentially creating new spawning grounds. Portal destruction must include environmental remediation to prevent residual spawning.

([[Dossier 03-0378.7233.4612]]: "Strategic Implications: Attrition Mathematics and Catalyst Dependency")

---

**VI. COUNTERMEASURES**

1. **Soul Supply Disruption:** Highest-priority countermeasure. The Dûrn Kordrek forge raid and remaining portal hunts ([[Dossier 01-0007.4737]]) directly target the soul-catalyst supply chain.
2. **Hezrou Targeting:** Develop a doctrine for prioritizing Hezrou kills in tactical engagements, particularly near known or suspected spawning areas.
3. **Terrain Sanctification:** Post-portal-destruction, deploy clerical teams to *Hallow* the corrupted terrain, rendering it permanently unsuitable for Dretch spawning. This is resource-intensive but prevents long-term residual gain.

([[Dossier 03-0378.7233.5738]]: "Countermeasure Recommendations: Soul Supply, Hezrou Targeting, Sanctification")
