**Subject:** Ziragz Portal Activity: Traffic Analysis and Node Mapping

---

**I. OVERVIEW**  
Ziragz serves as the **central logistics hub** for the Velvet Iron Army's portal network; a hub-and-spoke system with Ziragz at the center and subordinate portals distributed across the material plane and allied Abyssal layers. This dossier maps the network's known and suspected nodes, analyzes traffic patterns, and identifies exploitation opportunities.

---

**II. THE HUB; ZIRAGZ PORTAL COMPLEX**  
Ziragz contains four stabilized portals ([[Dossier 03-0380.3127]]):

| Portal | Class | Destination | Traffic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Central Gate | IV | Graz'zt's Triple Palace, Azzagrat | VIP and command communications only |
| Commerce Gate | III | Rotating destinations; currently linked to material plane trade points | Daily supply convoys |
| Military Gate | III | Rotation between frontline staging areas | Garrison relief, casualty evacuation |
| Processing Gate | III | Dûrn Kordrek forge complex ([[Dossier 19-3999.8638]]) | Soul product transit, jade amulet exchange |

The Commerce Gate is the most dynamic, its destination link is **re-tuned weekly** to cycle between multiple trade contact points, making it the most difficult to track. Known destination endpoints include the Suirx territories, the Khanate borderlands, and at least one location within the Preanese Theocracy's eastern parishes ([[Dossier 03-0380.6152.1108]]: "Commerce Gate Routing, Known Destinations and Re-Tuning Schedule").

**IV. TRAFFIC PATTERN ANALYSIS**

### Volume Estimates (Per Week):
- **Commerce Gate:** 50-80 tonnes of goods inbound (food, raw materials, trade goods); 10-20 tonnes outbound (manufactured goods, intelligence products)
- **Military Gate:** 200-400 fiendish personnel inbound/outbound (rotation cycles); casualty evacuation: 15-30 per week from the Stuik front
- **Processing Gate:** 40-60 jade amulet crates inbound (from material plane nodes); 10-20 soul-charged containers outbound (to Azzagrat)
- **Central Gate:** 1-3 transits per week; command staff only

### Temporal Patterns:
- Peak activity: Commerce and Processing Gates operate at maximum during the 3 days surrounding the weekly Commerce Gate re-tuning: a "market day" cycle.
- Military Gate peaks correlate with combat operations on the Stuik front: surges follow 48-72 hours after major engagements.
- Central Gate activity increases when Thraxxia is attending to business outside Ziragz: she has been absent from the city approximately 20% of the time over the past 6 months.

([[Dossier 03-0380.6152.3389]]: "Traffic Volume: Weekly Estimates and Temporal Patterns")

**V. NETWORK VULNERABILITIES**

1. **Single Hub Dependency:** All spoke nodes route through Ziragz. The hub-and-spoke model means that **disrupting Ziragz's portals would collapse the entire network**: spoke nodes cannot communicate with each other directly.
2. **Re-Tuning Window:** The Commerce Gate's weekly re-tuning creates a 4-6 hour window during which the portal is offline. During this window, Ziragz's food supply is interrupted and no trade goods can transit.
3. **Arcanaloth Bottleneck:** All portal maintenance requires Arcanaloth engineers. The Ascendant Watch estimates Thraxxia has access to approximately **8-12 Arcanaloths** total: a limited resource pool. Attrition of Arcanaloths ([[Dossier 03-0378.4338]]) directly degrades the network's maintenance capacity.
4. **Soul Product Pipeline:** The Processing Gate's soul-transit function is the highest-value traffic stream. Disrupting this pipeline denies Graz'zt the soul product he values most: potentially triggering direct intervention from the Dark Prince that could destabilize the alliance.

([[Dossier 03-0380.6152.4534]]: "Vulnerability Assessment: Hub Disruption, Re-Tuning Windows, and Engineer Attrition")

---

**VI. EXPLOITATION OPPORTUNITIES**

### A. Commerce Gate Interdiction
If the Commerce Gate's current material-plane endpoint can be identified, a strike team could intercept trade shipments; poisoning supplies, inserting disinformation, or tracking returning shipments to identify additional operatives.

### B. Processing Pipeline Disruption
Destroying the Dûrn Kordrek spoke node (the forge raid concept; [[Dossier 19-3999.8638]]) would eliminate the highest-volume input node on the Processing Gate, reducing soul product transit by an estimated 70%.

### C. Arcanaloth Targeting
Each Arcanaloth killed reduces the network's maintenance capacity. With only 8-12 available, losing 3-4 would create critical maintenance failures across the network.

([[Dossier 03-0380.6152.5671]]: "Exploitation Strategy: Commerce Interdiction, Processing Disruption, Arcanaloth Targeting")

**VII. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Network Mapping:** This is the most comprehensive map of the Velvet Iron Army's portal logistics network compiled to date. It reveals a system that is powerful but brittle: dependent on a single hub and a limited pool of specialist engineers.
2. **Priority Target:** The Arcanaloth pool is the network's most exploitable constraint. A sustained campaign of Arcanaloth elimination could degrade the entire portal network within months.
3. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Initiate a coordinated Arcanaloth attrition campaign across all known engagement points. Simultaneously, identify the Commerce Gate's current material-plane endpoint for interdiction planning. **[REDACTED]**
