**Subject:** Ziragz Defector Debriefs: Psychological Assessment and Compiled Testimony

---

**I. DEFECTOR OVERVIEW**  
The Ascendant Watch has successfully debriefed **three Ziragz defectors**; mortal citizens who escaped the floating city and reached material-plane allied territory. These individuals represent the only direct human-source intelligence on Ziragz's internal conditions. Their testimony is the foundation for multiple companion dossiers.

---

**II. DEFECTOR PROFILES AND ESCAPE CIRCUMSTANCES**

### Defector 1: "Shava" (Designation: BRIGHT-1)
**Real Name:** Maren Voss  
**Species:** Human female, age 31  
**Prior Status:** Jeweler in the Glass Ring, Ziragz resident for 14 months  
**Escape Method:** Bribery of a Cambion gate guard and passage through the Commerce Gate concealed within an outgoing trade shipment  

Maren is the most psychologically stable of the three defectors and has provided the most detailed and reliable testimony. Her background as a skilled artisan gave her broad social access within the Mortal Quarter.

Key intelligence contributions:
- Comprehensive layout of the Mortal Quarter residential blocks ([[Dossier 03-0380.3127]])
- Imp patrol schedule observations ([[Dossier 03-0380.6279]])
- Cold-room evasion technique: Maren independently discovered that imps avoid cold environments and used a deliberately unheated workshop for private conversations
- Description of the Compact of Gilt signing process and the moment she realized the identification medallion was a jade amulet

([[Dossier 03-0380.9735.1147]]: "BRIGHT-1 Full Debrief Transcript: Volumes I-III")

### Defector 2: "Welby" (Designation: BRIGHT-2)
**Real Name:** Torvald Krieger  
**Species:** Construct, age 44  
**Prior Status:** Prep cook in the Processing Quarter, Ziragz resident for 8 months  
**Escape Method:** Jumped from the platform edge with a *Feather Fall* scroll stolen from a Cambion officer's quarters  

Torvald's escape was extraordinarily dangerous and speaks to his desperation. He survived the 300-meter descent to the corrupted landscape below and wandered for approximately 48 hours before being located by a Lantern scout who detected his mortal aura against the Abyssal background.

Key intelligence contributions:
- Description of the Processing Quarter's soul-processing facilities ([[Dossier 03-0380.9735.3647]])
- Portal activity observations from the Garrison Quarter perimeter
- Description of a **mortal resistance group**: approximately 15 individuals who met secretly in the Processing Quarter's unfinished construction zones. This group was discovered and broken up by the imp network 3 weeks before Torvald's escape; 9 members were publicly executed

([[Dossier 03-0380.9735.2301]]: "BRIGHT-2 Full Debrief Transcript: Volumes I-II")

### Defector 3: "Serit" (Designation: BRIGHT-3)
**Real Name:** Serit Amund  
**Species:** Half-elf female, age 67  
**Prior Status:** Teacher in the Mortal Quarter school, Ziragz resident for 22 months; the longest-tenured known defector  
**Escape Method:** Recruited by an Ascendant Watch insertion operative and extracted through a pre-arranged Commerce Gate transit

Serit is the most damaged of the three defectors and the most valuable in terms of long-duration intelligence. She was a teacher in Ziragz's **mandated education program**; a curriculum designed by Thraxxia to instill Compact values in the mortal population's children.

Key intelligence contributions:
- Detailed description of the education curriculum, including its indoctrination elements
- Thraxxia's personal appearances in the Mortal Quarter: Serit observed Thraxxia directly on four occasions and provided physical descriptions, behavioral observations, and assessments of crowd reaction
- The existence and contents of the Compact's Infernal-language soul clause: Serit, literate in Infernal due to her half-elven education, was the first source to identify and translate the hidden provision ([[Dossier 03-0380.9849]])
- Information on the destroyed mortal resistance group: Serit knew three of the executed members personally

([[Dossier 03-0380.9735.3478]]: "BRIGHT-3 Full Debrief Transcript: Volumes I-IV")

**III. CROSS-CORROBORATION**  
All three defectors were debriefed independently. Cross-corroboration assessment:

- Physical layout descriptions: **95% consistent** across all three testimonies
- Imp network operations: **90% consistent**: minor discrepancies attributable to different sectors of observation
- Governance structure: **85% consistent**: Serit's longer tenure provides detail that contradicts some of Maren's assumptions but not observations
- Emotional assessments of Thraxxia: **divergent**: Maren views Thraxxia with hatred; Torvald with fear; Serit with a complex mixture of grudging respect and moral revulsion

([[Dossier 03-0380.9735.4612]]: "Cross-Corroboration Matrix: Agreement and Divergence Analysis")

**V. INTELLIGENCE GAPS; WHAT THE DEFECTORS DON'T KNOW**  
The defectors' intelligence is limited by their civilian perspective:

- None had access to the Garrison Quarter or the Gilt Tower interior
- None observed the Central Gate (Class-IV portal to Graz'zt) directly
- None can confirm the total mortal population count: estimates range from 6,000 (Maren) to 10,000 (Serit)
- None has intelligence on Thraxxia's personal capabilities, security arrangements, or decision-making process beyond public appearances

Filling these gaps requires either a higher-access defector (e.g., a Cambion administrator) or a dedicated insertion operation ([[Dossier 03-0380.9735.6893]]: "Intelligence Gaps: Priority Requirements and Collection Proposals").

---

**VI. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Invaluable Source:** These three defectors provide the only ground-truth intelligence on Ziragz's internal operations. Their protection is a high priority.
2. **Recruitment Potential:** The existence of a mortal resistance group (now destroyed) confirms that Ziragz residents are not uniformly compliant. Future resistance support is feasible if the imp network obstacle can be addressed.
3. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Protect all three defectors under maximum security cover identities. Continue Maren's analytical consultancy. Investigate channels for recruiting a higher-access defector from Ziragz's administrative tier. **[REDACTED]**
