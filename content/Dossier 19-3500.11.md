**CLASSIFIED: FOR CELESTIAL EYES ONLY**  
**INTELLIGENCE DOSSIER 19-3500.11**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Internal Security Division  
**Subject:** The Principality of Silesbia, Civil Unrest, and the Solstice Ball Assassination Attempt

---

**I. GEOGRAPHICAL & CLIMATE OVERVIEW**  
Silesbia occupies a narrow, heavily fortified corridor along the continent's western seaboard, wedged between the **Khanate of Tsayandakh** ([[Dossier 19-3189.0001]]) to the north and the **Kingdom of Stuik** ([[Dossier 19-3522.0001]]) to the south. The terrain is predominantly rolling hills and ancient forest, punctuated by deep river gorges and limestone cave networks that have been extensively developed into subterranean infrastructure.

Key features include:

- The **bunker-cities**: Silesbia's defining architectural achievement: twelve fortified population centers built partially or wholly underground, connected by reinforced tunnel networks. The largest, **Grauven**, houses approximately 120,000 inhabitants across six subterranean tiers.
- Surface terrain dominated by the **Greyloch Forest**, a dense old-growth woodland that has historically served as a natural defensive barrier against invasion from the east.
- A temperate maritime climate with heavy seasonal rainfall. The underground cities maintain stable temperatures year-round through geothermal venting systems.
- Rich deposits of iron, tin, and a rare crystalline mineral called **starite**, used in the manufacture of enchanted alloys. Silesbian metallurgy is considered second only to dwarven craftsmanship on the continent.

Current population: approximately **780,000**, distributed across the twelve bunker-cities and scattered surface settlements.

---

**II. GOVERNMENT & POLITICAL STRUCTURE**  
Silesbia operates as a constitutional principality under **Reagent Lunor**, a human noble of the ancient House Vethane. Lunor assumed the regency 14 years ago following the death of Princess Korvael during a tunnel collapse widely attributed to infernal sabotage ([[Dossier 19-3500.4368]]: "Investigation into the Korvael Collapse: Structural Analysis and Sabotage Indicators").

The government is structured as follows:

1. **The Reagent**: Acting head of state in lieu of a direct royal heir. Governs by consensus with the Iron Chamber. Lunor is known for his scholarly temperament and deep reluctance to commit Silesbian forces to the broader continental war.
2. **The Iron Chamber**: A legislative council of 24 noble houses, each representing one or more bunker-cities. Increasingly fractured between pro-war hawks (the **Ironist Faction**) and Lunor's pacifist loyalists (the **Shieldwall Caucus**).
3. **Bunker Wardens**: Military-administrative governors of each bunker-city, responsible for civil defense, resource allocation, and garrison command.
4. **The Deep Watch**: Silesbia's internal security force, numbering approximately 3,000. Tasked with tunnel defense, counter-espionage, and the suppression of smuggling operations. Increasingly stretched thin by rising civil unrest.

---

**III. LUNOR'S PACIFISM AND ITS CONSEQUENCES**  
Reagent Lunor has maintained a strict policy of **defensive neutrality** since the Velvet Iron Army's invasion began. His position, articulated in the *Doctrine of the Sealed Gate* (published 3 years prior), holds that Silesbia's bunker-cities are impregnable and that committing troops to the surface war would needlessly sacrifice Silesbian lives in a conflict that cannot be won through conventional arms.

This policy has produced the following consequences:

- **Military Stagnation:** Silesbia maintains a standing garrison of 29,000 soldiers, the majority of whom have not seen combat in over a decade. Morale assessments ([[Dossier 19-3500.7212]]: "Garrison Readiness Audit: Grauven, Tilvek, and Ossward") indicate deteriorating discipline and growing sympathies with the Ironist Faction among junior officers.
- **Refugee Crisis:** Silesbia has accepted approximately 95,000 refugees from the fallen Kingdom of Ulbgar ([[Dossier 19-3999.11]]) and contested eastern territories. The influx has strained food supplies and housing in the lower tiers of multiple bunker-cities, generating resentment among both native Silesbian populations and the refugees themselves.
- **Economic Isolation:** Trade disruptions caused by the war have crippled Silesbia's starite export economy. Warehouses in Grauven's upper tiers are filled with unsold mineral stock, while food imports from the Preanese Theocracy ([[Dossier 19-3611.11]]) have declined sharply: a reduction now understood to be partially engineered by compromised Preanese officials.

---

**IV. THE SOLSTICE BALL INCIDENT**  
On the night of the Winter Solstice, eighteen months ago, Reagent Lunor hosted the annual **Solstice Ball** in the Grand Gallery of Grauven; a diplomatic event attended by representatives of allied states, noble families, and senior military officials.

At approximately the 11th bell, a detachment of **Grauven Palace Guards**, later identified as members of a radicalized Ironist cell, attempted to assassinate the Reagent. The attack unfolded as follows:

1. **Perimeter Breach:** Six guards abandoned their exterior posts and sealed the Grand Gallery's blast doors from outside, trapping approximately 400 attendees within.
2. **Poisoning Attempt:** The Reagent's wine was laced with **deathcap nightsbloom**, a slow-acting paralytic. Lunor consumed the wine but was saved by a cleric of Ilmater ([[Dossier 19-3500.8964]]: "Witness Deposition: Brother Aldren of the Weeping Order") who recognized the symptoms and administered immediate treatment.
3. **Armed Assault:** The six rogue guards breached the Gallery through a service tunnel, armed with starite-edged weapons. They killed two members of the Reagent's personal bodyguard and wounded seven attendees before being subdued by loyal Deep Watch agents.
4. **Aftermath:** All six conspirators were captured alive. Under interrogation ([[Dossier 19-3500.3389]]: "Solstice Conspirators: Interrogation Transcripts"), they revealed connections to **Lord Vareth Telvaine**, a minor noble of the Ironist Faction. Telvaine fled Grauven before arrest and remains at large.

The Reagent survived but sustained injuries and has not appeared publicly since. His isolation has further emboldened the Ironist Faction, whose rhetoric has shifted from political opposition to open calls for Lunor's removal ([[Dossier 19-3500.3417]]: "Ironist Faction: Propaganda Analysis and Distribution Networks").

---

**V. INFERNAL EXPLOITATION**  
Our analysts assess with **moderate confidence** that the Velvet Iron Army has been actively encouraging Silesbian instability through indirect means:

- Intercepted communications ([[Dossier 19-3500.6195]]: "Decoded Transmissions; Telvaine Contact Network") suggest Lord Telvaine has received material support; including enchanted weapons and intelligence on Deep Watch patrol routes; from an intermediary linked to Thraxxia's operations in Ziragz ([[Dossier 03-0380.11]]).
- The refugee influx from Ulbgar may have been deliberately accelerated by infernal forces herding civilian populations toward Silesbian border crossings, overwhelming the principality's capacity and sowing internal discord.
- At least one Arcanaloth agent is believed to be operating within the Greyloch Forest, establishing relay portals for potential future incursion ([[Dossier 19-3500.5743]]: "Anomalous Arcane Signatures: Greyloch Southeast Quadrant").

---

**VI. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Objective:** Silesbia's bunker-cities represent the most defensible mortal strongholds on the continent. If mobilized, its garrison and engineering corps could provide critical support to Stuik's frontlines and potentially establish a western counteroffensive corridor.
2. **Current Status:** The principality is paralyzed by internal division. The Reagent is wounded and isolated. The Ironist Faction grows bolder but lacks unified leadership since Telvaine's flight. The Deep Watch is stretched between civil unrest, tunnel security, and counter-espionage.
3. **Vulnerability:** Continued inaction risks either a successful coup (installing an Ironist leader who may be unknowingly manipulated by infernal interests) or a collapse of civil order that renders the bunker-cities strategically irrelevant.
4. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Establish contact with moderate Ironist leaders who may be amenable to a controlled mobilization. Simultaneously, locate and neutralize the Arcanaloth operating in the Greyloch Forest before relay portals become operational. Priority: locate Lord Telvaine. **[REDACTED]**
