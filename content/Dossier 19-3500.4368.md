**CLASSIFIED: FOR CELESTIAL EYES ONLY**  
**REF. 19-3500.4368**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Forensic Engineering Division  
**Subject:** Investigation into the Korvael Collapse: Structural Analysis and Sabotage Indicators

---

**I. INCIDENT SUMMARY**  
Fourteen years ago, a catastrophic structural failure in **Tunnel Section 7-Gamma** of the Grauven bunker-city killed **Princess Korvael Vethane**, sole heir to the Silesbian throne, along with 23 members of her entourage and 14 civilian bystanders. The collapse buried a 120-meter stretch of primary transit tunnel under approximately 4,000 tonnes of limestone rubble.

The official Silesbian investigation, conducted by the Deep Watch Engineering Corps, concluded the collapse was caused by **natural geological stress fracture** exacerbated by seasonal groundwater infiltration. The ruling was issued within 30 days and the investigation sealed by order of the newly appointed Reagent Lunor.

This dossier presents the Ascendant Watch's independent assessment, which contradicts the official finding.

---

**II. STRUCTURAL ANALYSIS**  
Ascendant Watch engineering specialists, operating covertly as "visiting dwarven consultants", gained access to the collapse site three months after the incident. Their findings include:

### A. Fracture Pattern Anomalies
Natural geological collapses produce **radial fracture patterns** expanding outward from a central stress point. The Korvael Collapse exhibits a **linear shear pattern**, a clean, straight-line separation along the tunnel ceiling extending precisely 120 meters, with no radial deformation at either terminus ([[Dossier 19-3500.4368.1147]]: "Fracture Pattern Analysis, Photographic Evidence and Geological Comparison").

This pattern is consistent with a controlled cutting action; specifically, the application of sustained magical force along a predetermined line. It is inconsistent with any known natural geological failure mechanism.

### B. Residual Arcane Signatures
Spectral analysis of the collapsed rock detected trace **transmutation-school arcane residue** concentrated along the shear line. The residue's decay rate and signature profile are consistent with a **Stone Shape** or **Disintegrate** variant spell, cast at a power level exceeding standard mortal capability ([[Dossier 19-3500.4368.2289]]: "Arcane Residue Analysis: School, Potency, and Estimated Casting Timeframe").

The residue's age, corrected for ambient decay, dates the casting to **within 6 hours of the collapse**; far too recent to be attributed to historical construction magic.

### C. Princess Korvael's Itinerary
Korvael's presence in Tunnel 7-Gamma was **unscheduled**. Her official itinerary placed her in the Upper Grauven Council Chamber for a diplomatic reception. She diverted to 7-Gamma at the request of **Bureau-Chief Althus Reyne**, who suggested an impromptu inspection of ongoing infrastructure work.

Bureau-Chief Reyne survived the collapse, he departed the tunnel "to retrieve documentation" approximately four minutes before the event. He was not investigated. He died of apparent natural causes (heart failure) eight months after the collapse. No autopsy was performed ([[Dossier 19-3500.4368.3401]]: "Bureau-Chief Reyne, Timeline, Survival Circumstances, and Subsequent Death").

---

**III. SABOTAGE HYPOTHESIS**  
The Ascendant Watch assessment concludes with **high confidence** that the Korvael Collapse was a deliberately engineered assassination. The sabotage mechanism is assessed as follows:

1. **Preparation Phase:** An agent with access to the tunnel system, likely operating under magical concealment, applied a sustained transmutation-school spell along the ceiling shear line over a period of hours, weakening the structural integrity to a critical threshold.
2. **Trigger Phase:** A secondary activation, possibly time-delayed or remotely triggered, caused the final separation along the pre-weakened line, bringing 4,000 tonnes of rock down on the occupied section.
3. **Cover Phase:** Bureau-Chief Reyne lured the Princess to the prepared section and extracted himself before detonation. His subsequent death, plausibly attributed to stress-related natural causes, eliminated the most direct witness.

### Perpetrator Assessment
The casting power required exceeds mortal baseline. Possible perpetrators include:

- An **Arcanaloth** or comparable high-level fiendish spellcaster ([[Dossier 19-3500.4368.4538]]: "Arcanaloth Capability Assessment: Stone Manipulation Spells and Documented Precedents")
- A powerful mortal wizard operating under infernal contract
- A deployed infernal asset with specific structural sabotage training

The motive is assessed as **political destabilization**: Korvael's death eliminated the heir, triggered a succession crisis, and installed Reagent Lunor; whose pacifist philosophy has proven enormously beneficial to the Velvet Iron Army's strategic objectives.

---

**IV. REAGENT LUNOR; THE BENEFICIARY QUESTION**  
This assessment does **not** conclude that Reagent Lunor ordered or participated in the assassination. However, the following observations are noted:

- Lunor was next in the succession line as senior cousin of the Vethane bloodline. His appointment was immediate and uncontested.
- Lunor sealed the investigation and classified all evidence within 30 days: an action he justified as "preventing public panic" but which effectively precluded independent review.
- Lunor's subsequent policy decisions, the Doctrine of the Sealed Gate, military demobilization, cessation of arms exports, have, whether intentionally or not, served infernal strategic interests.

Whether Lunor is a willing collaborator, an unwitting beneficiary, or simply a pacifist whose philosophy aligns coincidentally with enemy objectives remains an open question ([[Dossier 19-3500.4368.5672]]: "Lunor: Motivation Analysis: Complicity, Manipulation, or Coincidence").

---

**V. INVESTIGATION STATUS**  
The Ascendant Watch's independent investigation remains active but constrained:

- Physical access to the collapse site was revoked when the "consulting dwarves" were politely escorted out of Silesbia. Subsequent access attempts have been blocked by Deep Watch security.
- Bureau-Chief Reyne's personal papers were confiscated after his death and placed in the Reagent's private archive: inaccessible without a warrant that only the Reagent can authorize.
- The Deep Watch's own engineers have privately expressed skepticism about the official geological stress finding, but none have been willing to speak on record ([[Dossier 19-3500.4368.6840]]: "Deep Watch Engineering: Informal Dissent and Information Barriers").

---

**VI. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Legacy Impact:** The Korvael assassination, if proven, would fundamentally alter the political landscape of Silesbia. Lunor's legitimacy rests on his succession; evidence of an engineered assassination that brought him to power would empower the Ironist Faction and potentially justify his removal.
2. **Double-Edged Sword:** Exposing the truth could mobilize Silesbia for war, but it could also trigger civil war if Lunor is perceived as complicit. The political fallout must be managed with extreme care.
3. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**, Do not release findings publicly at this time. Instead, use the intelligence as leverage for private negotiations with Lunor, confront him with the evidence and assess whether he is a willing participant or a man who simply cannot face the truth of how he came to power. His response will determine whether Silesbia can be turned into an ally or must be written off as a lost asset. **[REDACTED]**
