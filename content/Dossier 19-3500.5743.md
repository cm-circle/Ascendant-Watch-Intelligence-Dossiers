**CLASSIFIED: FOR CELESTIAL EYES ONLY**  
**REF. 19-3500.5743**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Arcane Surveillance Unit  
**Subject:** Anomalous Arcane Signatures: Greyloch Forest, Southeast Quadrant

---

**I. DETECTION SUMMARY**  
Lantern Scout designations LN-012 and LN-029 have detected **persistent anomalous arcane signatures** in the southeastern quadrant of the Greyloch Forest over a period of four months. The signatures are consistent with sustained **conjuration-school magic** of a potency associated with portal construction or maintenance.

The Greyloch Forest spans approximately 800 square kilometers of dense old-growth woodland between Silesbia and the eastern contested territories. It has historically served as a natural defensive barrier and trade corridor. The southeastern quadrant is the least-trafficked region, characterized by steep ravines, heavy canopy coverage, and limited trail infrastructure; ideal terrain for concealed operations.

---

**II. SIGNATURE ANALYSIS**  
The detected arcane emissions present the following characteristics:

- **School:** Conjuration, with secondary transmutation harmonics, consistent with portal anchor construction ([[Dossier 19-3500.5743.1182]]: "Arcane Emission Spectrum, Comparative Analysis with Known Portal Signatures")
- **Intensity:** Fluctuating between Class-II and Class-III equivalent. Peaks correlate with nighttime hours, suggesting active operational periods during darkness.
- **Source Localization:** Triangulation from three separate Lantern observation points places the emission source within a **2-square-kilometer area** centered on a deep ravine system designated **Ravine Thirteen** by Silesbian forestry maps. Ground penetration is extremely difficult due to dense undergrowth and unstable terrain.
- **Duration:** First detected four months ago. The persistence and gradual intensification of the signatures indicate an **ongoing construction project** rather than a completed and operational portal. Estimated time to operational status: 2-4 months ([[Dossier 19-3500.5743.2347]]: "Portal Construction Timeline: Estimated Completion Based on Emission Progression").

---

**III. SUSPECTED OPERATOR; ARCANALOTH ASSESSMENT**  
The construction of a stabilized portal requires **Arcanaloth-level arcane expertise** ([[Dossier 03-0378.11]]). Cross-referencing with intercepted communications from Lord Telvaine's network ([[Dossier 19-3500.6195]]; Transmission A-04) confirms that an Arcanaloth portal engineer has been deployed to the Greyloch Forest under Thraxxia's direction.

Additional evidence:

- Three Greyloch caravan attacks in the past six months ([[Dossier 19-3522.5758]]) occurred along routes passing within 5 kilometers of Ravine Thirteen. The attacks employed **conjured ice barriers** and **summoned shadow creatures**: tactics consistent with an Arcanaloth's spell repertoire but atypical for standard infernal patrol units.
- Forest wardens from the nearest Silesbian bunker-city (Ossward) have reported anomalous animal behavior in the southeastern quadrant, predator species avoiding the area entirely, songbird populations displaced, and a persistent "wrongness" in the air described by experienced rangers as "the smell before a thunderstorm, but constant" ([[Dossier 19-3500.5743.3489]]: "Forest Warden Reports, Wildlife Displacement and Atmospheric Anomalies").
- A Deep Watch patrol sent to investigate the animal displacement reports **never returned**. The five-member patrol entered the southeastern quadrant eleven weeks ago and has not been heard from since. The Deep Watch has not sent a follow-up patrol, the loss was classified as a "training accident" ([[Dossier 19-3500.5743.4612]]: "Missing Deep Watch Patrol, Circumstances and Official Misclassification").

---

**IV. TACTICAL ASSESSMENT; RAVINE THIRTEEN**  
Based on available intelligence, the operational picture at Ravine Thirteen is assessed as follows:

- **Primary Operator:** One Arcanaloth (CR 12), designated **"the Engineer"** in intercepted communications. The Arcanaloth is believed to be operating under a sustained invisibility and non-detection protocol, visible only during active spellcasting.
- **Security Detail:** An estimated 4-8 **Mezzoloths** (CR 5 each) serving as perimeter guards. Mezzoloth patrols have been indirectly detected through Lantern thermal scanning: multiple sub-baseline temperature signatures in a circular pattern around the emission center.
- **Infrastructure:** The portal anchor is likely being constructed within a natural cave or rock shelter in Ravine Thirteen's deepest section. Satellite construction may include supply caches, communication relay stones, and defensive wards.
- **Ley Line Exploitation:** The southeastern quadrant intersects a minor ley line, a natural channel of ambient arcane energy. The Arcanaloth is likely tapping this ley line to supplement its own casting resources, reducing the arcane expenditure (and therefore the detectable signature) required for portal construction ([[Dossier 19-3500.5743.5738]]: "Ley Line Survey, Greyloch Forest Arcane Geography").

---

**V. STRATEGIC IMPLICATIONS**  
An operational portal in the Greyloch Forest would represent a **catastrophic strategic threat** to Silesbia:

1. **Force Projection:** The portal would allow the Velvet Iron Army to deploy troops directly behind Silesbia's external defenses, bypassing the bunker-cities' fortified gatehouse complexes entirely.
2. **Infiltration Pipeline:** Even before military use, the portal would serve as a high-capacity insertion point for shapechangers, saboteurs, and intelligence operatives: the planned Deep Watch asset ([[Dossier 19-3500.6195]]) is likely the first of many.
3. **Supply Line Severance:** Portal-based forces operating from the Greyloch could cut the already-strained trade routes between Silesbia and Stuik, completing the logistical strangulation strategy ([[Dossier 19-3522.5758]]).
4. **Telvaine Connection:** The portal may serve as part of the route used to evacuate Telvaine to Ziragz: and could be used to insert him back into Silesbia as a controlled asset if Thraxxia chooses.

---

**VI. RECOMMENDED ACTION**

1. **Timeline Urgency:** The portal must be destroyed before reaching operational status. The intercepted spring equinox deadline ([[Dossier 19-3500.6195]]) provides an estimated 7-month window for action.
2. **Force Requirements:** Neutralizing an Arcanaloth and its Mezzoloth guard detail requires a capable strike team: minimum CR 12+ effective combat power. Mortal adventurers with anti-conjuration capabilities are preferred.
3. **Approach:** Ground infiltration through the forest is the recommended approach. Aerial approach would be detected by the Arcanaloth's divination wards. The strike team must be equipped with portal disruption charges ([[Dossier 01-0001.3214]]: "Project Quiet Gate") or sufficient destructive capacity to physically demolish the anchor pylons.
4. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Deploy a strike team to Ravine Thirteen with portal destruction as primary objective and Arcanaloth elimination as secondary. Coordinate timing with any planned intervention in Silesbian politics to maximize the strategic impact of exposing the portal as evidence of infernal operations on Silesbian soil. **[REDACTED]**
