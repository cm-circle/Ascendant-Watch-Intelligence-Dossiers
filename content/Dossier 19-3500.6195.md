**CLASSIFIED: FOR CELESTIAL EYES ONLY**  
**REF. 19-3500.6195**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Signals Intelligence Division  
**Subject:** Decoded Transmissions: Lord Telvaine's Contact Network

---

**I. COLLECTION SUMMARY**  
Following Lord Telvaine's flight from Grauven after the Solstice Ball incident ([[Dossier 19-3500.3389]]), the Ascendant Watch initiated a signals intelligence operation targeting Telvaine's known and suspected communication channels. Over a period of three months, **23 transmissions** were intercepted through a combination of enchanted relay monitoring, dead-drop surveillance, and arcane signal capture.

These transmissions reveal a sophisticated external support network linking Telvaine to **Thraxxia's intelligence apparatus** ([[Dossier 03-0380.11]]) through at least two intermediary layers.

---

**II. TRANSMISSION ANALYSIS; THE TELVAINE-AURIC CHANNEL**  
Twelve transmissions were exchanged between Telvaine and the intermediary designated **"Auric"** ([[Dossier 19-3500.3389]]). These communications were conducted through enchanted **paired rings**: a simple but effective system where spoken words into one ring are reproduced by the other. The rings have a limited range of approximately 50 kilometers and cannot cross planar boundaries.

Key decoded transmissions:

**Transmission T-03** (dated 6 days after Solstice Ball):  
*TELVAINE: "The operation failed. The cleric saved him. I need extraction."*  
*AURIC: "Extraction is not available at this time. Move east. I will establish contact at the Greyloch waystation."*

**Transmission T-07** (dated 18 days after):  
*TELVAINE: "I've reached the waystation. Where are you?"*  
*AURIC: "I am not coming. A package has been left for you: provisions, gold, and instructions. You are to proceed to the eastern forest edge. A guide will meet you. Do not deviate from the route."*

**Transmission T-11** (dated 31 days after):  
*TELVAINE: "The guide... what is he? He doesn't eat. He doesn't sleep. His eyes are wrong."*  
*AURIC: "He is a friend. Follow his instructions."*  
*TELVAINE: "Auric, where are you sending me?"*  
*AURIC: "Somewhere safe. Somewhere your talents will be appreciated. You will meet the Gilt Voice."*

"The Gilt Voice" is the handler designation identified in the Preanese decoded correspondence ([[Dossier 19-3611.5573]]), confirming a direct link between Telvaine's network and the Preas Cell ([[Dossier 19-3500.6195.1183]]: "Gilt Voice Cross-Reference, Telvaine and Preas Network Convergence").

**Transmission T-12** (final transmission; 34 days after):  
*TELVAINE: [Unintelligible: interference] "...the city floats... I can see it..."*  
*[Transmission terminated. Ring destroyed or moved beyond range.]*

Assessment: Telvaine was guided to **Ziragz**, Thraxxia's floating city ([[Dossier 03-0380.11]]). His current status is unknown; he is presumably alive and being debriefed, leveraged, or absorbed into Thraxxia's intelligence operations ([[Dossier 19-3500.6195.2347]]: "Telvaine, Probable Current Status and Intelligence Risk Assessment").

---

**III. THE AURIC-HANDLER CHANNEL**  
Seven transmissions were intercepted between Auric and an unidentified upstream handler; communications that Auric believed were secure but which were captured through experimental arcane relay monitoring. These transmissions used a variant of Cipher Beta ([[Dossier 19-3611.5573]]).

Key intelligence:

**Transmission A-02:**  
*HANDLER: "The Silesbian operation is acceptable despite tactical failure. The Ironists are more useful as a destabilizing force than as a successful coup. Ensure continued material support through alternate channels."*

**Transmission A-04:**  
*HANDLER: "A new asset is being prepared for insertion into the Deep Watch command structure. [Designation REDACTED] will arrive via the Greyloch portal within 30 days. Prepare receiving protocols."*  
*AURIC: "The Greyloch portal is not yet stable. The engineer reports interference from local ley line fluctuations."*  
*HANDLER: "Accelerate. The portal must be operational before the spring equinox."*

This confirms: (a) the Greyloch Forest Arcanaloth is constructing a portal under Thraxxia's direction, and (b) a new infiltration agent is being prepared for the Deep Watch, Silesbia's internal security force ([[Dossier 19-3500.6195.3514]]: "Deep Watch Infiltration Alert, Incoming Asset Warning").

**Transmission A-06:**  
*HANDLER: "The Reagent is more useful alive than dead. Revise all operational plans to preserve Lunor in office. His pacifism serves us better than any replacement would."*

This directive directly contradicts the Solstice Ball assassination attempt, suggesting either factional disagreement within the infernal command about Lunor's disposition, or a strategic recalculation following the attempt's failure ([[Dossier 19-3500.6195.4682]]: "Lunor Preservation Directive, Implications for Infernal Strategic Calculus").

---

**IV. THE MATERIAL SUPPORT PIPELINE**  
Four transmissions detail the logistics of material support for the Ironist Faction:

- **Weapons:** Starite-edged weapons are sourced from a diverted Silesbian arms shipment: specifically, a consignment originally destined for Stuik export that was "lost in transit" six months ago ([[Dossier 19-3522.5758]]). The weapons were rerouted through the Greyloch waystation.
- **Gold:** Funding for Ironist operations (broadsheet printing, safe houses, tavern network operations) is laundered through a legitimate Silesbian mining concern; **Keldrek Minerals Ltd.**; whose majority shareholder is a shell entity registered in the Suirx Trade Company Territories ([[Dossier 19-3500.6195.5817]]: "Keldrek Minerals; Corporate Structure and Infernal Financial Ties").
- **Intelligence:** Auric has been providing the Ironists with accurate intelligence on Deep Watch patrol schedules and garrison rotations, information that could only have been obtained through a source within the Deep Watch itself, predating the new asset mentioned in Transmission A-04 ([[Dossier 19-3500.6195.6203]]: "Pre-existing Deep Watch Compromise, Evidence and Scope Assessment").

---

**V. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Network Confirmation:** Telvaine's network is confirmed as an arm of Thraxxia's intelligence operations, linked through "Auric" and "the Gilt Voice" to the broader Preas Cell and Ziragz command structure.
2. **Telvaine's Fate:** Telvaine is almost certainly in Ziragz. He possesses detailed knowledge of Silesbian military dispositions, political dynamics, and Iron Chamber proceedings. His debriefing by Thraxxia's intelligence staff represents a significant intelligence loss for Silesbia.
3. **Immediate Threats:** The incoming Deep Watch infiltration asset and the Greyloch portal timeline represent urgent, actionable threats requiring immediate countermeasures.
4. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**, Priority 1: Alert FOB Ashwatch to the incoming Deep Watch asset, intercept or identify before insertion. Priority 2: Disrupt the Greyloch portal before the spring equinox deadline. Priority 3: Investigate Keldrek Minerals for financial intelligence on the broader infernal funding network. **[REDACTED]**
