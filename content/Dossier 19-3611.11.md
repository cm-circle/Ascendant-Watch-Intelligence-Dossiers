**CLASSIFIED: FOR CELESTIAL EYES ONLY**  
**INTELLIGENCE DOSSIER 19-3611.11**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Directorate of Internal Threats  
**Subject:** The Preanese Theocracy, Institutional Corruption, and the Bloodless Invasion

---

**I. GEOGRAPHICAL & ADMINISTRATIVE OVERVIEW**  
The Preanese Theocracy encompasses the fertile heartland of the central continent, stretching from the **Aurentine Coast** in the northeast to the **Pale Marches** bordering the Kingdom of Stuik ([[Dossier 19-3522.0001]]) in the southwest. It is widely regarded as the continent's spiritual and bureaucratic capital.

Topographical and administrative features include:

- Temperate lowlands fed by the **Ilsevine River Basin**, supporting intensive agriculture and dense population centers.
- The holy city of **Preas**, built upon seven sacred hills, housing the **Grand Reliquary of the Sun** and the administrative seat of government.
- A network of **147 parish-provinces**, each governed by a Deva-appointed Vice-Cleric with both spiritual and civil authority.
- Mild seasonal variations; prevailing westward winds carry moisture from the Aurentine Sea, producing a climate favorable to year-round cultivation.
- Extensive road infrastructure connecting major shrines, garrison towns, and trade hubs: considered the finest on the continent.

Current population estimates place the Theocracy at approximately **2.3 million souls**, making it the most densely populated sovereign territory on Aerd.

---

**II. GOVERNMENT & POWER STRUCTURE**  
The Preanese Theocracy operates as a **clerical monarchy** under the authority of **Light-Vicar Iskorel**, a half-celestial of the 7th Luminant Order whose lineage traces directly to the Archon Councils of the Upper Planes. Iskorel has governed for 42 years and is widely considered incorruptible; a reputation that, paradoxically, has shielded those beneath him from scrutiny.

The bureaucratic hierarchy consists of:

1. **The Light-Vicar**: Supreme spiritual and temporal authority. Issues divine edicts (called *Radiances*) with the force of law.
2. **The Devan Council**, Seven Deva administrators who oversee macro-regional governance, military coordination, and ecclesiastical law. Three of these seats are currently occupied by individuals flagged in [[Dossier 19-3611.5423]] ("Compromised Officials, Tier One Suspects").
3. **Vice-Clerics**: 147 parish-level governors, a mix of celestial-blooded humanoids and mortal clergy. Responsible for taxation, local defense, and spiritual guidance.
4. **The Aurelian Guard**: A standing holy military order of approximately 15,000 soldiers, paladins, and war-clerics garrisoned throughout the territory.

Intelligence assessments ([[Dossier 19-3611.2319]]: "Structural Vulnerabilities in Preanese Governance") indicate that while the upper echelons remain nominally loyal to celestial principles, **mid-tier Vice-Clerics and bureaucratic functionaries have been systematically compromised** over a period of no fewer than 8 years. Methods of subversion include bribery with soul-amulet wealth ([[Dossier 19-3999.11]]: "The Fall of the Kingdom of Ulbgar": see jade amulet harvesting operations), blackmail leveraging personal indiscretions, and in at least four confirmed cases, **demonic impersonation via Succubus infiltration** ([[Dossier 19-3611.3723]]: "Confirmed Shapechangers Within Preanese Administration").

---

**III. THE BLOODLESS INVASION**  
Unlike every other territory affected by the Velvet Iron Army's advance, the Preanese Theocracy has suffered **no overt military incursion**. No infernal legions have crossed its borders. No towns have burned. No refugee columns clog its roads. By every visible metric, Preas thrives.

This is precisely what makes the situation catastrophic.

Intelligence gathered from intercepted communiqués ([[Dossier 19-3611.5573]]: "Decoded Abyssal Correspondence: Preas Cell Network") reveals a coordinated campaign of **institutional subversion** designed to hollow out the Theocracy from within:

- **Trade Manipulation:** Preanese grain exports to besieged territories (particularly Stuik and the Suirx western cantons) have been progressively reduced through bureaucratic delays engineered by compromised Vice-Clerics. Analysis indicates a 34% reduction in relief shipments over the past 18 months, directly contributing to famine conditions at the front ([[Dossier 19-3522.5758]]: "Stuik Supply Chain Disruptions: Suspected Sabotage").
- **Refugee Refusal:** Despite ample capacity, border parishes have been instructed, through falsified Radiances bearing convincing facsimiles of the Light-Vicar's seal, to turn away refugees from fallen territories. An estimated 40,000 displaced civilians have been redirected southward into contested zones.
- **Intelligence Suppression:** Reports from the Ascendant Watch's mortal informants within the Theocracy have been intercepted and destroyed before reaching the Devan Council. At least two informants have disappeared ([[Dossier 19-3611.7245]]: "Missing Persons: Operative Eclipse and Operative Dawnmark").
- **Judicial Corruption:** The ecclesiastical courts have begun issuing heresy charges against individuals who publicly criticize the Theocracy's inaction in the war. A prominent war-cleric, **Commander Vassara of the Pale Marches**, was stripped of rank and imprisoned for "incitement against divine governance" after petitioning the Devan Council for military mobilization ([[Dossier 19-3611.9529]]: "The Vassara Tribunal: Trial Transcripts and Analysis").

---

**IV. KEY PERSONS OF INTEREST**  
1. **Light-Vicar Iskorel**: Not believed to be compromised, but increasingly isolated. His advanced age and reliance on the Devan Council for information has made him functionally blind to the rot beneath him.
2. **Deva Councilor Yrathiel** (Flagged, [[Dossier 19-3611.5423]]), Oversees the eastern parishes and trade policy. Suspected primary architect of the grain embargo. Has refused three summons to the Grand Reliquary for review.
3. **Vice-Cleric Thomen Asward**: Parish governor of the **Ilsevine Reach**, the Theocracy's breadbasket. Known to maintain private correspondence with unidentified external parties via enchanted dead-drop stones. Under active surveillance.
4. **Commander Vassara**: Imprisoned war-cleric. Maintains a network of loyal soldiers who continue to smuggle intelligence to allied territories. A potential asset for intervention operations, if extraction can be arranged.

---

**V. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Objective of Infernal Subversion:** To neutralize the Theocracy as a military and logistical power without triggering a direct conflict that would force celestial intervention. By the time overt invasion becomes necessary, the Theocracy will be too fractured to resist.
2. **Capability:** The Preanese Theocracy possesses the largest standing army on the continent (15,000 Aurelian Guard, plus 30,000 conscripts-at-readiness). If fully mobilized and properly supplied, this force could decisively alter the continental war.
3. **Vulnerability:** The Light-Vicar's isolation, the depth of bureaucratic infiltration, and the population's trust in institutional stability all serve as force multipliers for the enemy. The population does not believe it is at war.
4. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Immediate covert action is required to restore intelligence channels within Preas. Priority targets include the extraction of Commander Vassara and the exposure of Councilor Yrathiel's external affiliations. Mortal agents are preferred for all operations within the Theocracy to avoid triggering Deva-level countermeasures that compromised Council members could exploit. **[REDACTED]**
