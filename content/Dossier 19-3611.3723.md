**CLASSIFIED: FOR CELESTIAL EYES ONLY**  
**REF. 19-3611.3723**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Shapechanger Detection Unit  
**Subject:** Confirmed and Suspected Shapechangers Operating Within the Preanese Administration

---

**I. OVERVIEW**  
This dossier documents the confirmed and suspected presence of **fiendish shapechangers**, primarily Succubi and Incubi, operating within the Preanese Theocracy's government apparatus. Shapechanger infiltration represents one of the most difficult-to-detect vectors of infernal subversion, as these entities can perfectly replicate the physical appearance, voice, and surface mannerisms of their targets.

Detection protocols employed by the Ascendant Watch rely on a combination of:

- **Thermal anomaly scanning** by Lantern scouts (fiends generate subtly different heat signatures from mortals, detectable at ranges under 15 meters)
- **Behavioral deviation analysis** through long-term observation by embedded mortal agents
- **Divination-based confirmation**, which requires proximity and carries significant risk of detection by the target
- **Aura reading** by celestial operatives, limited by the detection threshold constraints ([[Dossier 01-0001.11]])

To date, the unit has identified **four confirmed** and **seven suspected** shapechangers operating within the Preanese bureaucracy. This dossier covers the confirmed cases in detail and the suspected cases in summary.

---

**II. CONFIRMED SHAPECHANGER; CASE FILE ALPHA**  
**Target Replaced:** Aldris Venn, Private Secretary to Deva Councilor Myreth  
**Entity Type:** Succubus (manifesting as male human: "Incubus" variant)  
**Confirmation Method:** Thermal anomaly + behavioral deviation + intercepted communications  
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED

The entity occupying Venn's identity arrived approximately 18 months ago following the death of the previous secretary ([[Dossier 19-3611.5423]]). Thermal scans conducted by Lantern Scout designation LN-047 on three separate occasions recorded core body temperature of 33.1°C, 3.5°C below human baseline, consistent with fiendish physiology under sustained illusion ([[Dossier 19-3611.3723.1103]]: "Thermal Scan Records, Subject Venn, Dates and Methodology").

The entity has successfully maintained its cover by:

1. Studying the original Venn's personal correspondence (seized from his private quarters prior to replacement)
2. Limiting social interactions to professional contexts where behavioral deviations are less noticeable
3. Engineering the reassignment of three colleagues who knew the original Venn closely

Estimated duration of infiltration: 18 months. The original Aldris Venn is presumed dead; no body has been recovered.

---

**III. CONFIRMED SHAPECHANGER; CASE FILE BETA**  
**Target Replaced:** Seal-Clerk Orenthas, Office of the Celestial Seal  
**Entity Type:** Succubus  
**Confirmation Method:** Behavioral deviation + aura anomaly  
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED

Orenthas was replaced approximately 11 months ago. The replacement entity is more sophisticated than the Venn infiltrator; its behavioral mimicry is nearly flawless. Detection was achieved through a chance observation by Deva Sentinel Koravael (FOB Dawnspire; [[Dossier 01-0001.6424]]) during a diplomatic visit to Preas, who noted a "wrongness" in Orenthas's aura that his formal celestial senses could not explicitly identify ([[Dossier 19-3611.3723.2287]]: "Koravael Incident Report; Aura Anomaly Observation, Orenthas").

Subsequent thermal scanning confirmed sub-baseline core temperature. The original Orenthas was an Aasimar; a being with celestial heritage; making the impersonation particularly audacious. The replacement entity is suspected of using a modified illusion that incorporates a faint divine-energy emitter to simulate Aasimar aura characteristics ([[Dossier 19-3611.3723.3401]]: "Divine-Energy Emitter Hypothesis; Technical Analysis and Countermeasure Development").

---

**IV. CONFIRMED SHAPECHANGER; CASE FILE GAMMA**  
**Target Replaced:** Courier-Captain Lysara Trent, Radiance Courier Network (Central Hub)  
**Entity Type:** Incubus  
**Confirmation Method:** Thermal anomaly + physical evidence (original body discovered)  
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED

Trent commanded the central routing hub for the Radiance Courier Network in Preas, controlling which couriers received which routes and managing the seal-verification process for incoming dispatches. The original Trent's body was discovered by Ascendant Watch mortal operatives in a hidden sub-basement beneath the courier headquarters, preserved through low-level necromantic stasis, apparently kept as a reference for ongoing impersonation needs ([[Dossier 19-3611.3723.4534]]: "Trent Body Recovery, Forensic Analysis and Concealment Methods").

The Incubus replacement has been in position for approximately **seven months**. During this period, courier route assignments have been systematically altered to direct sensitive dispatches through interception-vulnerable corridors ([[Dossier 19-3611.2319]]; see courier network vulnerabilities).

The body recovery provides the first physical evidence of shapechanger activity within Preas. However, the body has not yet been extracted; doing so would alert the infiltrator. Current recommendation: leave the body in place and use the knowledge to feed disinformation through the compromised courier network.

---

**V. CONFIRMED SHAPECHANGER; CASE FILE DELTA**  
**Target Replaced:** Vice-Cleric Hamund Bryce, Parish of Eastmere  
**Entity Type:** Succubus  
**Confirmation Method:** Behavioral deviation + trade pattern analysis  
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED

Eastmere is a small coastal parish of approximately 8,000 inhabitants. The original Vice-Cleric Bryce was a notably pious and reclusive administrator. The replacement entity, installed approximately 14 months ago, has dramatically altered the parish's trade policies, opening unauthorized shipping lanes that analysis suggests are being used for jade amulet importation ([[Dossier 19-3611.3723.5671]]: "Eastmere Shipping Anomalies: Trade Pattern Analysis and Amulet Trafficking Indicators").

Unlike the other confirmed cases, the Eastmere infiltration appears to serve primarily as a **logistics node** rather than an intelligence-gathering position. The parish's coastal location and low profile make it ideal for smuggling operations beneath institutional notice.

**VII. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Scale of Infiltration:** Four confirmed shapechangers in key positions represents a significant and coordinated intelligence operation. The positions targeted, information gatekeeping, courier routing, judicial administration, and logistics, suggest a deliberate infrastructure of subversion rather than opportunistic placement.
2. **Detection Gap:** Current detection methods are resource-intensive and require proximity. The Ascendant Watch lacks the capacity to screen all 147 Vice-Clerics and their senior staff. The seven suspected cases likely represent a fraction of total infiltration.
3. **Countermeasure Development:** Research into area-effect detection methods; specifically, a modified *Detect Evil and Good* ritual that could screen large gatherings; is underway but faces the detection-threshold constraint ([[Dossier 19-3611.3723.7942]]: "Mass Detection Protocol; Feasibility Study and Energy Requirements").
4. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Immediate priority: confirm or clear S-07. If the Light-Vicar's physician is compromised, the Light-Vicar himself may be under direct physical manipulation. Secondary: extract Courier-Captain Trent's body and replace the infiltrator with a turned asset or loyal replacement to establish a controlled disinformation channel. **[REDACTED]**
