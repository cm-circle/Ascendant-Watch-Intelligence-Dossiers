**CLASSIFIED: FOR CELESTIAL EYES ONLY**  
**REF. 19-3611.5573**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Signals Intelligence Division  
**Subject:** Decoded Abyssal Correspondence: The Preas Cell Network

---

**I. COLLECTION OVERVIEW**  
This dossier compiles and analyzes **47 intercepted communications** between infernal agents operating within the Preanese Theocracy and their external handlers. The messages were collected over a period of eleven months through three independent collection methods:

1. **Dead-drop seizure:** Physical recovery of enchanted correspondence stones discarded after use by Vice-Cleric Thomen Asward and associates ([[Dossier 19-3611.5423]]). Magical residue on the stones permitted post-facto reconstruction of transmitted messages ([[Dossier 19-3611.5573.1108]]: "Enchanted Stone Forensics: Residue Extraction Methodology and Reliability Assessment").
2. **Courier interception:** Seventeen dispatches intercepted from the Radiance Courier Network through compromised route segments ([[Dossier 19-3611.2319]]: see courier vulnerabilities). These documents were copied and returned to the courier stream to avoid alerting senders.
3. **Arcane signal capture:** Experimental detection of short-range magical transmissions emanating from Councilor Yrathiel's eastern complex in Aurevast. This method is unreliable and has produced only fragments, but the fragments corroborate patterns identified through other collection streams ([[Dossier 19-3611.5573.2247]]: "Aurevast Signal Intercepts: Technical Limitations and Fragment Analysis").

---

**II. CIPHER SYSTEMS AND DECRYPTION**  
The intercepted communications employ three distinct cipher systems:

- **Cipher Alpha:** A substitution cipher using Infernal script mapped to Common phonemes. Relatively simple; broken within two days of first contact. Used primarily for low-priority logistical messages.
- **Cipher Beta:** A polyalphabetic system keyed to lunar cycles, requiring knowledge of both the current Abyssal calendar and a rotating keyword. Broken after three months of sustained cryptanalysis ([[Dossier 19-3611.5573.3391]]: "Cipher Beta: Cryptanalysis Methodology and Key Recovery"). Used for operational instructions and personnel directives.
- **Cipher Gamma:** An arcane-encrypted system that embeds messages within seemingly innocuous trade invoices. Decryption requires both the correct dispelling sequence and a physical key-object (a specific jade amulet). Only **four Gamma-encrypted messages** have been successfully decrypted; an estimated 12-15 remain unbroken ([[Dossier 19-3611.5573.4513]]: "Cipher Gamma: Partial Decryption and Outstanding Intelligence Gaps").

---

**III. KEY INTELLIGENCE DERIVED FROM DECODED MESSAGES**

### A. Network Structure Confirmation
The communications confirm the hub-and-spoke infiltration model documented in [[Dossier 19-3611.5423.]] Specific revelations include:

- The network's external handler is designated **"the Gilt Voice"**, almost certainly a Cambion operative reporting to Thraxxia ([[Dossier 03-0380.11]]). The handler communicates through Cipher Beta messages routed via the Eastmere coastal parish ([[Dossier 19-3611.3723]], Case File Delta).
- The network comprises at least **three operational tiers**: Tier 1 (Yrathiel, Myreth/Venn; strategic direction), Tier 2 (Asward, corrupted Vice-Clerics; operational execution), and Tier 3 (Voranthi, Orenthas, Trent replacements; information control).

### B. Operational Directives
Decoded Cipher Beta messages include the following directives from the Gilt Voice:

1. "*Reduce grain flow by increments not exceeding 5% per month. Abrupt curtailment invites scrutiny.*": Dated nine months ago. Confirms the grain embargo is externally directed rather than internally motivated.
2. "*The blind one must remain comfortable. His health is our shield.*": Dated seven months ago. "The blind one" is assessed as a reference to the Light-Vicar (alluding to the blindfold symbolism of Tyr, whose iconography permeates Preanese culture). This directive suggests the network has standing orders to keep Iskorel alive but isolated.
3. "*Prepare the eastern parishes for transition. Phase designation: Gilt.*", Dated three months ago. "Phase Gilt" is consistent with Phase Three of the Velvet Iron Army operational doctrine ([[Dossier 03-0378.11]]), post-conquest governance under Thraxxia's model. This implies planning for the eventual overt absorption of the Theocracy into Thraxxia's domain.

### C. Personnel Transfers
Cipher Alpha messages document the movement of at least **twelve infernal agents** through the Theocracy over the past year, transiting via the Stios portal (now destroyed; [[Dossier 01-0007.11]]) and secondary routes. Agent designations include:

- "Goldfinch": deployed to the Grand Reliquary, mission unknown
- "Ashworm": deployed to the Pale Marches, assessed as a counter-surveillance operative targeting FOB Dawnspire ([[Dossier 01-0001.6424]])
- "Silkthread": deployed to Aurentine Port, mission assessed as logistics coordination

Full agent designation list and deployment analysis: [[Dossier 19-3611.5573.5687]]: "Infernal Agent Deployment Manifest: Decoded Entries and Gap Analysis".

### D. The Gamma Messages
The four successfully decrypted Gamma messages are the most sensitive intelligence in this collection. They include:

1. A request for **additional shapechangers**, specifically, "two individuals capable of sustained celestial-aura mimicry", suggesting plans to replace additional Deva Council members or high-ranking celestial-blooded officials.
2. A progress report on the **Light-Vicar's physician** (S-07 per [[Dossier 19-3611.3723]]), confirming the physician's role in administering a "compliance tincture" that reduces Iskorel's cognitive acuity and willingness to question received information.
3. A financial accounting of jade amulet distribution within the network: quantities sufficient to maintain addiction in **23 confirmed dependents** across the Theocracy's bureaucracy.
4. A contingency protocol designated **"Sunfall"**, instructions for the rapid collapse of the network and evacuation of key assets in the event of exposure. The protocol includes the assassination of six mortal collaborators who possess too much knowledge to be left alive ([[Dossier 19-3611.5573.6824]]: "Protocol Sunfall, Decoded Contingency Plan and Target List").

---

**IV. INTELLIGENCE GAPS**  
Significant gaps remain:

- The twelve unbroken Cipher Gamma messages likely contain the most strategically valuable intelligence. Dedicated cryptanalysis resources are required ([[Dossier 19-3611.5573.4513]]).
- The identity and location of "the Gilt Voice" remain unknown. Direction-finding on the handler's transmissions has been attempted but the enchanted communication stones do not emit traceable signals.
- The full scope of the "Phase Gilt" transition plan for eastern parishes has not been recovered.

---

**V. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Value:** This signals intelligence collection represents the single most comprehensive window into infernal operations within the Theocracy. It has confirmed the network's structure, validated suspect identities, and revealed the long-term strategic objective of Phase Gilt absorption.
2. **Risk:** Continued collection depends on the network not discovering that its communications have been compromised. Any overt action against identified suspects must be weighed against the potential loss of this intelligence stream.
3. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Maintain passive collection. Dedicate additional cryptanalysis resources to the remaining Gamma messages. Use the Protocol Sunfall intelligence to prepare countermeasures: if the network attempts to self-destruct, the assassination targets must be extracted first. **[REDACTED]**
