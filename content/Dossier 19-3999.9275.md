**CLASSIFIED: FOR CELESTIAL EYES ONLY**  
**REF. 19-3999.9275**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Arcane Material Analysis Laboratory  
**Subject:** Jade Amulet Properties: Alchemical, Psychometric, and Soul-Harvesting Analysis

---

**I. SPECIMEN COLLECTION**  
The Ascendant Watch has acquired **fourteen jade amulet specimens** through various collection operations:

- 4 specimens recovered from the Stios Portal operation ([[Dossier 01-0007.11]])
- 3 specimens seized from Preanese Vice-Cleric Asward's private quarters ([[Dossier 19-3611.5423]])
- 3 specimens recovered from fallen Stuik soldiers ([[Dossier 19-3522.5758]])

All ten specimens are currently housed in a **warded containment vault** at FOB Ashwatch. Handling protocols require divine protection wards and a minimum of two clerical observers during any examination.

---

**II. PHYSICAL PROPERTIES**  
The amulets are remarkably uniform in manufacture, confirming industrial-scale production ([[Dossier 19-3999.8638]]):

- **Material:** Nephrite jade, pale green with occasional darker veins. Spectral analysis matches Ulbgari deep-stratum jade deposits.
- **Dimensions:** Oval disc, approximately 4cm × 3cm × 0.8cm. Smooth-polished surface.
- **Weight:** 28-31 grams, with weight variance correlating to soul-charge level (see Section IV).
- **Carving:** Each amulet bears a central sigil on one face: a spiral-within-a-spiral motif identified as the Abyssal glyph for "container" or "treasury." The reverse face bears a unique serial identifier in Yugoloth technical notation.
- **Attachment:** All amulets are set in a simple gold bezel with a chain loop. The gold is not decorative, it serves as a **conductive pathway** for the soul-harvesting process ([[Dossier 19-3999.9275.1108]]: "Gold Conductor Analysis, Microstructure and Energy Flow Patterns").

---

**III. SOUL-CHARGE INDICATORS**  
Amulets vary in soul-charge level, which can be assessed through weight and luminescence:

| Charge Level | Weight Variance | Luminescence | Soul Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| Empty (new) | 28g | None | No soul energy |
| Partial | 29-30g | Faint green glow in darkness | Fragments from active users |
| Full | 31g+ | Steady green pulse | One or more complete captured souls |
| Processed | 28g (reset) | None | Soul extracted by Arcanaloths |

Full-charge amulets are prioritized for collection by the infernal logistics network. Processed amulets are recycled into the distribution chain.
