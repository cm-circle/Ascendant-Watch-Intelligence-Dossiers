**THREAT ASSESSMENT 19-4002.11**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Extremism Monitoring Division  
**Subject:** Radicalized Sects Within the Followers of Tyr, Mortal Alliance with Undead Agents, and Implications for the Continental War

---

**I. BACKGROUND; THE FAITH OF TYR ON AERD**  
**Tyr**, the Blind God of Justice, is one of the most widely venerated deities on the continent, worshipped across multiple territories including the **Preanese Theocracy** ([[Dossier 19-3611.11]]), the **Kingdom of Stuik** ([[Dossier 19-3522.0001]]), and pockets within **Silesbia** ([[Dossier 19-3500.11]]). His doctrine emphasizes **absolute impartial justice**, the punishment of the guilty, and the protection of the innocent. In peacetime, Tyran clergy serve as judges, inquisitors, and legal advocates. Their temples double as courthouses.

The invasion has placed extraordinary strain on the Tyran faithful. The core crisis is theological: Tyr's doctrine demands that the guilty be punished and the innocent defended: yet the celestial response has been one of measured restraint ([[Dossier 01-0001.11]]: "Celestial Intervention Protocols"). To many rank-and-file Tyran clerics, this restraint is indistinguishable from inaction. Children are being slaughtered, kingdoms are falling, and the gods appear to do nothing.

This theological dissonance has produced **radicalization**; a growing movement within Tyr's clergy that has abandoned the institutional faith in favor of **extremist direct action**.

---

**II. THE RADICALS; STRUCTURE AND IDEOLOGY**  
The radicalized sects; collectively referenced as the **"Unblinded"**; operate as a loose network of independent cells rather than a unified organization. Intelligence estimates identify approximately **1,200-1,800 active Unblinded** across the continent, with the heaviest concentrations in the eastern territories closest to the frontlines ([[Dossier 19-4002.4992]]: "Unblinded Cell Mapping; Known and Suspected Locations").

Core ideological tenets:

- **Tyr's blindfold is a chain, not a symbol.** The Unblinded embrace Tyr's traditional blindfold imagery, interpreting it not as impartial justice but as willful ignorance. ([[Dossier 19-4002.5583]]: "Unblinded Initiation Rites: Documented Practices and Symbolic Analysis").
- **Justice demands action, not faith.** The Unblinded have declared the established Tyran clergy and the celestial hierarchy complicit in the suffering of innocents through inaction. They consider the Ascendant Watch's restraint doctrine a betrayal.
- **Any tool that serves justice is righteous.** This is the most dangerous tenet. The Unblinded have explicitly abandoned the prohibition against allying with evil-aligned entities if such alliances serve the destruction of the Velvet Iron Army. This includes the use of necromancy, cooperation with undead agents, and the formation of tactical partnerships with entities that would normally be considered enemies of the faith.

The most prominent Unblinded leader is **Hammerjudge Serevka Ironblind**, a former High Inquisitor of Tyr's temple in the Stuik border fortress of **Trevnor**. Serevka was expelled from the formal clergy after she executed three suspected infernal collaborators without trial, citing "emergency divine authority." She now commands the largest confirmed Unblinded cell, estimated at 300 combatants ([[Dossier 19-4002.2927]]: "Profile: Hammerjudge Serevka Ironblind: History, Capabilities, and Threat Assessment").

---

**III. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Immediate Threat Level:** The Unblinded are assessed as a **moderate and growing threat** to both the war effort and the post-war order. Their tactical effectiveness is real but comes at an unacceptable strategic cost.
2. **The Schism's Cost:** The fracturing of Tyr's faith weakens one of the continent's strongest moral and institutional pillars. If the Unblinded movement continues to grow, it will undermine the legitimacy of all clerical institutions, making post-war reconstruction significantly more difficult.
3. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**, A dual approach is required. First: identify and engage moderate Unblinded elements who may be amenable to reintegration into the formal Tyran hierarchy under reformed terms, Hammerjudge Serevka is a potential interlocutor if approached correctly. **[REDACTED]**
