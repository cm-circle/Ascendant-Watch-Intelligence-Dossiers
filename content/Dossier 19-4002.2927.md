**CLASSIFIED: FOR CELESTIAL EYES ONLY**  
**REF. 19-4002.2927**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Personality Intelligence  
**Subject:** Profile: Hammerjudge Serevka, Leader of "The Anvil"

---

**I. IDENTIFICATION**  
**Name:** Serevka (surname unknown; possibly abandoned at ritual)  
**Species:** Human female, age approximately 38  
**Title:** Hammerjudge (supreme leader) of Cell 1, "The Anvil"  
**Location:** Ossward, Silesbia; operates from a converted granary in the refugee quarter  
**Physical:** Tall, lean, weathered complexion. Missing left eye (Band of the Maimed). Prominent scarring on both forearms: old combat injuries. Carries a consecrated warhammer ("Tyrant's End") and wears battered half-plate under a dark wool cloak.

---

**II. BACKGROUND**  
Before the Unblinded, Serevka was a **War-Priestess of Tyr** serving in the Stuik 2nd Field Army's clerical contingent. She participated in 14 engagements on the Stuik front over 2 years, earning a reputation as an effective battlefield healer and a ferociously brave frontline combatant.

Her conversion to the Unblinded was triggered by the **Battle of Redhollow**; an engagement where her unit was ordered to abandon a fortified village to reposition along a more defensible line. The village's 300 civilian inhabitants were left to the advancing Dretch column. Serevka attempted to disobey orders and defend the village independently; she was physically restrained by her commanding officer and forced to retreat.

All 300 villagers were killed. Serevka deserted the following month.

She performed the Rite of Clarity alone in a Silesbian forest, arrived in Ossward 3 weeks later, and within 6 months had recruited the 35-member Anvil cell from Ulbgar refugees and Stuik deserters.

([[Dossier 19-4002.2927.1147]]: "Background: Military Service, Redhollow, and Conversion")

---

**III. CAPABILITIES**  
Serevka is assessed as the most dangerous individual in the Unblinded movement:

- **Divine Casting:** 5th-level clerical spellcasting (War Domain). Access to *Spirit Guardians*, *Crusader's Mantle*, and full combat cleric suite. Her connection to Tyr remains strong despite her heretical status: Tyr appears not to have rescinded her divine mandate.
- **Combat:** Experienced frontline combatant. Proficient with warhammer, shield, and improvised weapons. Has personally killed at least 4 fiendish entities (2 Mezzoloths, 1 Cambion, 1 Babau) in Anvil operations along the Silesbian border.
- **Leadership:** Charismatic and decisive. Members of the Anvil describe her as "the only leader who has never asked us to do something she hasn't done first." High personal loyalty among her followers.
- **Tactical Acumen:** Serevka's military experience translates into effective small-unit tactics. The Anvil operates with discipline unusual for a vigilante group: proper reconnaissance, fallback positions, and after-action review.

([[Dossier 19-4002.2927.2301]]: "Capabilities Assessment: Combat, Casting, and Leadership")

---

**IV. BEHAVIORAL ANALYSIS**

### Strengths:
- Genuine moral conviction: not power-seeking or self-aggrandizing
- Disciplined despite radicalism: will not sacrifice her people unnecessarily
- Effective motivator: her origin story (Redhollow) resonates with refugee trauma

### Vulnerabilities:
- **Guilt complex:** The Redhollow incident drives her. Every civilian death she cannot prevent reinforces the original trauma. She is susceptible to manipulation through threatened civilian populations.
- **Institutional hatred:** Her rage at the Stuik command that ordered the Redhollow retreat extends to all institutional authority. She will reject cooperation with any force she perceives as "another system that abandons the innocent."
- **The DJLMF question:** Serevka has met the DJLMF entity ([[Dossier 19-4002.7263]]): the encounter is described by Anvil members as "tense but respectful." Serevka's private position on the DJLMF is unknown and presents a potential fracture point within the Anvil.

([[Dossier 19-4002.2927.3478]]: "Behavioral Analysis: Strengths, Vulnerabilities, and DJLMF Contact Assessment")

---

**V. RELATIONSHIP WITH THE ASCENDANT WATCH**  
Serevka is **unaware** of the Ascendant Watch's existence. She operates under the assumption that no celestial forces are active in the theater; a belief that reinforces her conviction that mortals must fight their own war.

If she were to discover the Ascendant Watch's Restraint Doctrine, that celestial forces are present but choosing not to intervene, her reaction would likely be **explosive fury**. The knowledge that celestial beings watched while Redhollow burned would confirm her deepest grievance and could drive her toward even more extreme action.

**Assessment:** Direct contact with Serevka is HIGH RISK. If contact is ever initiated, it must be through a mortal intermediary who can frame the Ascendant Watch's role in a way that does not trigger her institutional betrayal complex.

([[Dossier 19-4002.2927.4612]]: "Ascendant Watch Contact Risk: Scenario Assessment")

---

**VI. INFLUENCE AND TRAJECTORY**  
Serevka's influence extends beyond Cell 1:
- Cell 2 (Blind Justice) considers her the "spiritual leader" of the movement, even though no formal command relationship exists
- Cell 4 (Children of the Maimed God) was founded by a former Anvil member who traveled to the Suirx territories at Serevka's suggestion
- The Unblinded's growth rate (15-20/month) is disproportionately concentrated around Ossward: Serevka's personal charisma is the primary recruitment engine

**Trajectory:** If the Unblinded continue to grow, Serevka will become the de facto leader of a movement with hundreds of members. At that scale, the Unblinded transition from a vigilante sect to a genuine insurgency. Whether this insurgency fights demons or destabilizes allied nations depends on Serevka's decisions in the coming months.

([[Dossier 19-4002.2927.5738]]: "Influence Mapping and Trajectory Projection")

---

**VII. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Key Individual:** Serevka is the single most important mortal in the Unblinded movement. Her decisions will determine whether the Unblinded become an asset or a disaster.
2. **Handle with Care:** Any attempt to control, suppress, or manipulate Serevka will backfire. She is driven by genuine moral conviction and will detect instrumentalization.
3. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Monitor Serevka closely but do not initiate contact. If circumstances create a natural opportunity for cooperation (e.g., convergent operations against a shared infernal target), allow events to create the relationship rather than forcing it. **[REDACTED]**
