## The Recovered Page: Full Translation

_(Upper portion, Personal Entry, Ancient Abyssal)_

> _"I, who was blind and made others blind, have completed my course. What was stored up for me is a crown of cold justice that no living hand may place. I was given the power once to see truth, but what I saw I could not endure and so I covered the eyes of those who followed me, that they need not see it either. My days run out not in failure but in purpose. What I do now, I do knowing full well the name of what I serve. It is not Tyr. It has never been Tyr."

---

_(Lower portion, Formal Contract, Ancient Abyssal, different hand)_

---

**LET IT BE INSCRIBED IN THE OLD TONGUE THAT NO LIVING MOUTH MAY SPEAK:**

**Between:** The one called DJLMFAO, formerly of the Blind Court, Keeper of the Frost Doctrine, Master of the Veiled - hereafter _the Instrument_

**And:** The Rule of Three, Voice of the Graceful Dark, Third Mouth of the Prince of Pleasure, He Who Speaks in Conditions - hereafter _the Arbiter_

**On behalf of:** Graz'zt, Prince of the Argent Palace, Lord of the Triple Layer, He Whose Reflection Does Not Lie - hereafter _the Patron_

---

**ARTICLE I: PURPOSE**

The Instrument agrees to disrupt, interdict, and destroy diplomatic vessels operating along the western sea lanes during the period of the Infernal Advance, with particular attention to vessels carrying persons of political significance to the free cities of the interior. The Instrument shall not act in the name of Hell's armies, nor fly any colors, nor leave survivors capable of coherent testimony.

---

**ARTICLE II: COMPENSATION**

In exchange for services rendered, the Patron guarantees the following:

- Safe passage of the Instrument and his Veiled through all infernal blockade lines, by sea or by summoning
- Upon the Instrument's final destruction, which the Patron acknowledges is both inevitable and acceptable, the Instrument's essence shall not be claimed by Tyr's judicial dead, but shall pass instead into the Argent Palace as a _guest of the court_, neither tormented nor freed, for a period no shorter than one thousand years

---

**ARTICLE III: CONDITIONS**

The Rule of Three, speaking for the Patron, attaches the following conditions:

1. The Instrument shall take no action against fiendish assets operating in the same sea lanes. What Hell builds, the Instrument shall not unmake.
2. The journal. _This journal._ Shall be destroyed before or upon the Instrument's final destruction. It shall not fall into mortal hands.
3. Should the Instrument fail to destroy the journal, the third condition applies: **the Patron assumes no liability for what its contents set in motion.**

---

**ARTICLE IV: ACKNOWLEDGMENT**

_The Instrument has read the conditions. The Instrument has signed below.

`✦ — [mark of the Instrument, frost-burned into the page]`
`✦✦✦ — [mark of the Rule of Three, in triplicate, pressed in silver-black ink]`

---

_The contract is witnessed by no one. That is the point._
