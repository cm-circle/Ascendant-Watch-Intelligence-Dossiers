**REF. 19-4002.4992**  
**Origin:** Ascendant Watch: Radical Movements Analysis  
**Subject:** The Unblinded: Cell Mapping and Distribution

---

**I. OVERVIEW**  
The **Unblinded** are a radical sect of Tyr worshippers who believe that the god of justice demands **active, violent resistance** against infernal forces; rejecting the institutional Preanese Theocracy's diplomatic and defensive approach as spiritual cowardice. They identify themselves through the ritual removal of one eye; symbolizing Tyr's own sacrifice; and refer to their sighted eye as the "Eye of Judgment."

The Ascendant Watch has identified **six active cells** operating across the theater. This dossier maps their locations, estimated membership, and assessed capability.

---

**II. CELL REGISTER**

### Cell 1: "The Anvil" (Ossward, Silesbia)
**Membership:** ~35  
**Leader:** Hammerjudge Serevka ([[Dossier 19-4002.2927]])  
**Capability:** HIGH; the largest and best-organized cell, with access to weapons, armor, and divine spellcasters  
**Activities:** Armed patrols of the Silesbian borderlands; confrontation with suspected Ironist sympathizers; violent interrogation of captured jade amulet distributors  
**Status:** Active and expanding; recruiting aggressively from Ulbgar refugees in the Ossward camps

([[Dossier 19-4002.4992.1108]]: "Cell 1: The Anvil, Detailed Profile and Activity Log")

### Cell 2: "Blind Justice" (Stuik Frontier Towns)
**Membership:** ~20  
**Leader:** Justicar Brennan Holt  
**Capability:** MODERATE; mostly veterans from the Stuik militia, equipped with personal weapons and some divine casting ability  
**Activities:** Vigilante raids on suspected infernal collaborators behind the Stuik lines; execution of a Cambion operative captured near Halvegård Tower (unconfirmed); distribution of anti-Thraxxia propaganda  
**Status:** Active but under Stuik military surveillance; they operate in the security seams between garrison towns

### Cell 3: "The Scourge" (Preas, Eastern Parishes)
**Membership:** ~15  
**Leader:** Unknown; operational security is unusually tight  
**Capability:** LOW to MODERATE; small but fanatically devoted  
**Activities:** Targeted assassinations of suspected infernal operatives within the Preanese Theocracy. The Scourge is credited with killing 2 individuals who were later confirmed as Cambion infiltrators; providing accidental intelligence validation.  
**Status:** Hunted by both the Preanese civil authorities (who consider them heretics) and infernal assets (who consider them a threat)

([[Dossier 19-4002.4992.2247]]: "Cells 2-3: Profiles and Activity Logs")

### Cell 4: "Children of the Maimed God" (Suirx Territories)
**Membership:** ~10  
**Leader:** Sister Kalliste (former nun of a Waukeen temple who converted after witnessing jade amulet effects)  
**Capability:** LOW; small, poorly equipped, but positioned in critical territory  
**Activities:** Jade amulet destruction campaigns; recovering and destroying amulets found in refugee camps; confrontational preaching against Ziragz recruitment ([[Dossier 03-0380.6922]])  
**Status:** Active but vulnerable; operating in Thraxxia's primary recruitment zone

### Cell 5: "The Scales" (Stuik Capital)
**Membership:** ~25  
**Leader:** Magistrate-Cleric Torund Ashe (a serving Stuik judicial official who has not publicly revealed his affiliation)  
**Capability:** MODERATE; well-connected in Stuik institutional circles, but focused on political action rather than violence  
**Activities:** Political advocacy for aggressive war policy; organizing civilian support for Stuik military operations; legal prosecution of suspected collaborators through the Stuik courts  
**Status:** The most "respectable" cell: their activities are legal (if aggressive) and their methods non-violent. However, Ashe's private correspondence reveals increasing frustration with legal channels and interest in the Anvil's more direct methods.

### Cell 6: "The Blade Unseen" (Location Unknown)
**Membership:** Estimated 5-8  
**Leader:** Unknown  
**Capability:** UNKNOWN; no direct intelligence  
**Activities:** Attributed with 3 assassinations of mid-ranking fiendish agents across the theater, but attribution is uncertain. This cell, if it exists, operates with complete operational security.  
**Status:** Existence is assessed as PROBABLE but not confirmed. May be a propaganda fabrication by other cells to create the impression of a wider network.

([[Dossier 19-4002.4992.3389]]: "Cells 4-6: Profiles and Activity Logs")

**IV. COORDINATION ASSESSMENT**  
The six cells operate with **minimal inter-cell coordination**. The Unblinded lack a unified command structure; each cell is autonomous, led by its own Hammerjudge or equivalent authority. Some communication exists between Cell 1 (Anvil) and Cell 2 (Blind Justice) through travelers along the Silesbia-Stuik corridor, but operational planning is not shared.

The DJLMF entity ([[Dossier 19-4002.7263]]) has made contact with at least Cell 1 and Cell 3, but the nature of this contact is disputed; some Unblinded consider the DJLMF an ally; others consider it an abomination.

([[Dossier 19-4002.4992.4534]]: "Inter-Cell Coordination: Communication Links and Command Absence")

---

**V. STRATEGIC ASSESSMENT**

1. **Asset or Liability:** The Unblinded are simultaneously the most committed anti-infernal force outside official military structures and the most likely to cause collateral damage through uncontrolled violence.
2. **Intelligence Value:** Cell 3's accidental intelligence validation (killing confirmed Cambions) demonstrates that the Unblinded's targets sometimes overlap with Ascendant Watch priorities.
3. **Risk:** Uncontrolled Unblinded violence risks destabilizing allied institutions (Preanese, Stuik) and providing propaganda material for Thraxxia.
4. **Recommendation:** **[REDACTED]**: Monitor all cells. Consider covert engagement with Cell 5 (the legal-focused cell) as a potential intelligence-sharing channel. Avoid direct contact with Cell 1 (Anvil) until their DJLMF relationship is better understood. **[REDACTED]**
